# 🥊 Showdown Saturdays — Weekly Drummer Battle Series

**Mission:** Drive follower growth toward 5K Thomann threshold through weekly drummer gear comparison polls.

## Overview

Every Saturday, we pit two legendary metal drummers against each other in a gear showdown. Fans vote on whose setup they prefer, driving massive engagement through the tribal nature of metal fandom.

## Why It Works

1. **Tribal Engagement** — Metal fans are loyal. They'll rally for their favorite drummer.
2. **Poll Mechanics** — Twitter polls drive high engagement (shares, quote tweets, comments)
3. **Recurring Format** — Weekly consistency builds anticipation
4. **Debate Fuel** — "Who's better" debates are engagement gold in metal communities
5. **Cross-Platform** — Same matchup can run on Twitter, Instagram Stories, and TikTok

## Series Structure

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Saturday 10:00 | Poll goes live (24h duration) |
| Saturday 12:00 | Story/TikTok support content |
| Saturday 18:00 | Engagement boost (quote tweet, reply to comments) |
| Sunday 10:00 | Poll closes, announce winner |
| Sunday 12:00 | Deep-dive thread on winning drummer's gear |
