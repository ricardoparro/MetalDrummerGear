## Matchup Selection Criteria

### High-Engagement Matchups
1. **Same band, different eras** (Joey vs Jay)
2. **Direct style comparison** (Gene vs George - speed)
3. **Classic rivalries** (Lars vs Lombardo)
4. **Generational debates** (Old school vs new school)

### Medium-Engagement Matchups
1. **Same genre, different bands** (Tomas vs Mario)
2. **Similar technique focus** (Double bass specialists)
3. **Gear brand debates** (Pearl drummers vs Tama drummers)

### Avoid
- Drummers with no overlap in fanbase
- Obscure matchups (save for special editions)
- Too similar = boring results
