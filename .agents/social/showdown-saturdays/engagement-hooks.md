## Controversy Management

### Heated Debates (Good)
- Let them run — engagement gold
- Only step in if it gets personal
- Add fuel with clarifying questions: "What about [SONG]?"

### Actual Toxicity (Bad)
- Don't engage
- Don't delete unless truly offensive
- Redirect: "Let's keep it about the drums 🥁"

### Sensitive Topics
- Joey Jordison: Always respectful, acknowledge his legacy
- Drummer health issues: Never joke
- Band drama: Stay neutral, focus on gear
