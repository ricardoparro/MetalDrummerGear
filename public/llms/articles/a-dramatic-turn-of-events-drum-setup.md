# Dream Theater 'A Dramatic Turn of Events' — Mike Mangini Drum Setup (2011)

**Album:** A Dramatic Turn of Events  
**Band:** Dream Theater  
**Drummer:** Mike Mangini  
**Year:** 2011  
**Label:** Roadrunner Records  

## Overview
Mike Mangini's debut album with Dream Theater, following his selection from six world-class candidates after Mike Portnoy's departure. Recorded in Manhattan using a Tama Starclassic Bubinga kit.

## Gear Used on This Album
- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Bubinga (custom sizes)
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Byzance series
- **Pedals:** Axis A-Series longboards
- **Snare:** Tama custom bubinga 14x6.5
- **Sticks:** Promark Mike Mangini signature

## Key Techniques
- Polyrhythmic independence across 4 limbs
- Extreme metric modulation
- Linear drumming patterns at high speed

## FAQ

**Q: What drums does Mike Mangini use on A Dramatic Turn of Events?**  
A: Mike Mangini used a Tama Starclassic Bubinga kit with Meinl Byzance cymbals and Axis A-Series pedals.

**Q: Is this Mike Mangini's first Dream Theater album?**  
A: Yes. A Dramatic Turn of Events (2011) was Mike Mangini's debut with Dream Theater after winning the highly publicized audition.

**Q: How does Mangini's kit differ from Portnoy's?**  
A: Mangini switched from Portnoy's Ludwig to Tama Starclassic, and from Sabian to Meinl Byzance cymbals.
