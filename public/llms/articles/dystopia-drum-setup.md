# Megadeth 'Dystopia' — Dirk Verbeuren Drum Setup (2016)

**Album:** Dystopia  
**Band:** Megadeth  
**Drummer:** Dirk Verbeuren  
**Year:** 2016  
**Label:** Universal Music  

## Overview
Grammy Award winner for Best Metal Performance (2017). Dirk Verbeuren's debut with Megadeth following Chris Adler's guest appearance on earlier recordings. His first studio album with the band solidified the Soilwork drummer as a thrash metal force.

## Gear Used on This Album
- **Drums:** Sonor SQ2 custom maple
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Pure Alloy / Byzance series
- **Pedals:** Sonor Giant Step double pedal
- **Snare:** Sonor custom 14x5
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth Dirk Verbeuren signature

## Key Techniques
- Thrash metal double-bass patterns at 220+ BPM
- Technical fill work bridging thrash and modern metal
- Tight pocket playing complementing Dave Mustaine's riffs

## FAQ

**Q: What drums does Dirk Verbeuren use on Dystopia?**  
A: Dirk Verbeuren used a Sonor SQ2 custom maple kit with Meinl cymbals and Sonor Giant Step pedals.

**Q: Did Dirk Verbeuren win a Grammy for Dystopia?**  
A: Yes — Dystopia won Best Metal Performance at the 59th Grammy Awards (2017).

**Q: What is Dirk Verbeuren's snare setup?**  
A: On Dystopia, Verbeuren used a Sonor custom 14x5 snare drum.
