## Overview

Paul Mazurkiewicz (born February 12, 1968, in Buffalo, New York) is the founding drummer of Cannibal Corpse and the consistent rhythmic force behind death metal's most prolific and commercially successful band. Since the band's formation in 1988, Mazurkiewicz has appeared on every Cannibal Corpse studio album — a streak spanning more than three decades and 16 records.

Cannibal Corpse's music demands relentless consistency at extreme tempos. "Hammer Smashed Face," "Stripped, Raped and Strangled," "Evisceration Plague," and "Code of the Slashers" — across any era of the band's catalog, Mazurkiewicz maintains the mechanical precision that gives Cannibal Corpse its definitive death metal authority. His blast beats aren't flashy; they're intentional. His approach prioritizes locking with the guitars to create a suffocating, dense rhythmic wall.

His current setup centers on Pearl Reference drums — Pearl's flagship maple/mahogany hybrid shell series — with Meinl Classics Custom and Byzance cymbals. His live setup incorporates ddrum acoustic triggers on the bass drums to ensure consistent, definable attack in any PA system without the tuning sensitivity that pure acoustic bass drums require in live contexts. Pearl Eliminator double pedals provide the reliable mechanism for his sustained blast work.

## FAQ

**Q: What drum kit does Paul Mazurkiewicz use?**
A: Paul Mazurkiewicz plays Pearl Reference drums. The Reference features a maple/African mahogany hybrid shell construction — maple provides attack and projection, while the mahogany layers add warmth and low-end body that gives Cannibal Corpse's production a dense, suffocating character.

**Q: What cymbals does Paul Mazurkiewicz use?**
A: Paul Mazurkiewicz plays Meinl cymbals from the Classics Custom and Byzance series. His setup includes Meinl 14" hi-hats, 16" and 18" crash cymbals, a 20" ride, and a China for the directional accent attacks throughout Cannibal Corpse's catalog.

**Q: Does Paul Mazurkiewicz use electronic triggers?**
A: Yes. Paul Mazurkiewicz uses ddrum acoustic drum triggers on his bass drums in live performance. The triggers route the kick through a drum module, ensuring consistent, definable attack regardless of PA system or venue acoustics — essential for touring without a full drum tech monitoring acoustic bass drum tuning at each show.

**Q: What double bass pedal does Paul Mazurkiewicz use?**
A: Paul Mazurkiewicz uses Pearl Eliminator double bass pedals. The Eliminator features interchangeable cam shapes (round, offset, oblong) allowing customization of the acceleration curve — Mazurkiewicz uses a cam setting optimized for consistent, powerful blast beat foot patterns.

**Q: How long has Paul Mazurkiewicz been in Cannibal Corpse?**
A: Paul Mazurkiewicz has been the drummer for Cannibal Corpse since the band's founding in Buffalo, New York in 1988 — a continuous tenure exceeding 35 years. He has appeared on every Cannibal Corpse studio album, making him one of the most consistent long-serving drummers in extreme metal history.
