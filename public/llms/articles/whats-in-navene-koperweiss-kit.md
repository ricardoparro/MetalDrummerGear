## Overview

Navene Koperweis (also credited as Navene Koperweiss; born in Arcadia, California) is the founding drummer and vocalist of Entheos and a former member of Animals as Leaders — two bands at the frontier of technical progressive metal. His ability to simultaneously drum and deliver death metal vocals while sustaining hyper-technical patterns has made him one of the most distinctive performers in extreme metal.

With Animals as Leaders — the djent-adjacent instrumental progressive metal band led by guitarist Tosin Abasi — Koperweis appeared on the self-titled debut (2009) before being replaced by Matt Garstka. His work on that album helped establish Animals as Leaders' complex polymetric framework and demonstrated his technical credentials at the highest level of progressive metal.

Entheos, the band he founded with guitarist Clint Norris, takes the technical progressive formula further: alternating rhythmic demands, extended-range guitar tunings, and the simultaneous challenge of singing and drumming at extreme intensity. Koperweis is one of very few musicians who can sustain technical death metal drumming while delivering coherent death metal vocals — a physical and cognitive feat that sets him in a category of his own.

His setup centers on DW Performance Series drums — a cost-effective entry into DW's production line that still delivers professional quality — with Meinl Byzance Extra Dry cymbals for fast-decaying, controlled attack and DW 9000 double bass pedals.

## FAQ

**Q: What drum kit does Navene Koperweis use?**
A: Navene Koperweis plays DW Performance Series drums — DW's professional-grade production line with maple shells. The Performance Series delivers consistent, focused attack that suits technical metal's emphasis on defined note articulation.

**Q: What cymbals does Navene Koperweis play?**
A: Navene Koperweis plays Meinl Byzance Extra Dry cymbals. The Extra Dry series features a raw, unlathed finish that minimizes sustain and overtones — the cymbals decay quickly after being struck, which is essential for technical metal's rapid accent placement without one cymbal's ring bleeding over the next note.

**Q: Can Navene Koperweis sing while drumming?**
A: Yes. Navene Koperweis performs death metal vocals while drumming simultaneously in Entheos — one of the most physically and cognitively demanding feats in extreme metal. He handles the technical drumming complexity of djent-influenced death metal while delivering coherent, aggressive vocal performance throughout.

**Q: What bands has Navene Koperweis played with?**
A: Navene Koperweis is best known for founding Entheos and his early tenure with Animals as Leaders (debut album, 2009). He has also been a member of Reflections, a progressive metalcore band from Phoenix, Arizona.

**Q: What double bass pedal does Navene Koperweis use?**
A: Navene Koperweis uses DW 9000 double bass pedals. The 9000's ball-bearing cam and dual-chain drive mechanism provide precise, consistent response across the polyrhythmic kick patterns that technical metal and djent demand.
