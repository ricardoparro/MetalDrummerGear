## Drummer(s)

**Navene Koperweis** — 2010–2012
Navene Koperweis was Animals as Leaders' early drummer, recording *Weightless* (2011), a djent-genre landmark. He later founded Entheos and co-produced the band's *The Joy of Motion* (2014) after his departure, while also running his own solo project Fleshwrought and producing electronic music as "Navene K."

[Navene Koperweis full profile →](https://metalforge.io/drummer/navene-koperweis) · [LLM markdown](https://metalforge.io/llms/drummers/navene-koperweis.md)

**Matt Garstka** — 2012–present
Matt Garstka, a Berklee-trained drummer, replaced Navene Koperweis in 2012 and has recorded every Animals as Leaders album since, including *The Joy of Motion* (2014), *The Madness of Many* (2016), and *Parrhesia* (2022). His jazz-fusion-informed linear independence, ghost-note vocabulary, and use of traditional grip in a metal context have made him one of the most influential progressive metal drummers of his generation.

## Signature Sound

Animals as Leaders is an instrumental progressive metal act built entirely around complex, technical composition — its drumming has to carry the melodic weight normally handled by vocals. Navene Koperweis established the band's djent-genre-defining rhythmic vocabulary on *Weightless* (2011). Matt Garstka's arrival brought a jazz-fusion sophistication layered onto that foundation, with linear independence and metric modulation that made *The Joy of Motion* (2014) and *The Madness of Many* (2016) technical benchmarks for progressive metal drumming.
