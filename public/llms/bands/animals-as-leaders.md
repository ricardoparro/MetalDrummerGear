## Drummer(s)

**Navene Koperweis** — 2010–2012
Navene Koperweis, born May 31, 1985 in San Jose, California, is an American drummer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist known for his work with progressive metal band Entheos and his earlier tenure with Animals as Leaders. His drumming combines extreme technical precision with creative musical expression across djent, deathcore, and electronic music. Beyond drumming, Koperweis is a prolific producer who releases electronic music as "Navene K" and runs his own technical death metal project Fleshwrought, where he plays all instruments.

**Matt Garstka** — 2012–present
Matt Garstka, born April 27, 1989 in Westfield, Massachusetts, is an American drummer known for his virtuosic work with Animals as Leaders since 2012. His playing blends jazz fusion, electronic music, and progressive metal, featuring complex polyrhythms, intricate ghost note patterns, and innovative use of dynamics. Garstka joined after Koperweis's departure, bringing a jazz-educated sensibility built on traditional grip, linear independence, and metric modulation that has made him one of the most influential drummers of his generation.

## Signature Sound

Animals as Leaders' drumming has always centered on technical virtuosity within an instrumental progressive metal framework. Navene Koperweis brought extreme technical precision and creative musical expression from his background spanning djent, deathcore, and electronic production. Since 2012, Matt Garstka has expanded that foundation with a jazz-fusion-informed approach — polyrhythms, ghost note patterns, and dynamic control built on traditional grip and metric modulation. Both drummers gravitate toward Meinl Byzance cymbals for their dark, complex overtones, giving the band's technical compositions a cohesive tonal signature even as the players behind the kit changed.
