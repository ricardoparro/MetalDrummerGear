## Drummer(s)

**Vinnie Paul** — 2006–2018 (Founding member)
Vinnie Paul (1964–2018) founded the groove metal supergroup Hellyeah in 2006, his final full-time band after Pantera and Damageplan. He drummed on the self-titled 2007 debut and every subsequent Hellyeah album, bringing the same powerful, groove-locked style that had defined Pantera's *Cowboys from Hell* and *Vulgar Display of Power* two decades earlier. Vinnie Paul passed away in 2018; the posthumous *Welcome Home* (2019) was his final recorded album with the band.

## Signature Sound

Hellyeah gave Vinnie Paul a platform to continue the groove metal foundation he built with Pantera, this time alongside members of Mudvayne, Nothingface, and (Hed) PE. His powerful, pocket-focused drumming — emphasizing feel and groove over technical display — anchored the band's self-titled 2007 debut and its Southern-metal-tinged follow-ups, carrying his signature sound into a new generation of heavy music until his death in 2018.
