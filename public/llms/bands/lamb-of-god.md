## Drummer(s)

**Chris Adler** — 1994–2019
Chris Adler is the co-founder and longtime drummer of Lamb of God, one of the most successful American metal bands of the 21st century. His innovative approach to groove metal drumming, combining technical precision with crushing heaviness, has made him one of the most influential drummers in modern metal. His drumming is characterized by powerful groove-based patterns, creative use of dynamics, and signature techniques like his "pivot" hi-hat foot work. He departed Lamb of God in 2019 and has since focused on new projects and drum education.

**Art Cruz** — 2019–present
Art Cruz has been the drummer for Lamb of God since 2019, following Chris Adler's departure. His powerful, precise drumming has seamlessly integrated with Lamb of God's crushing groove metal sound while bringing his own energy to the band's performances. Before Lamb of God, Cruz built his reputation through extensive work with Winds of Plague and Prong, developing a reputation as a reliable, hard-hitting drummer capable of handling diverse metal styles. His work on the band's 2020 self-titled album and 2022's "Omens" has proven him a worthy successor to Adler's legacy.

## Signature Sound

Lamb of God's drumming foundation was built by co-founder Chris Adler, whose powerful groove-based patterns, dynamic control, and signature "pivot" hi-hat technique defined the band's rise across *Ashes of the Wake*, *Sacrament*, and *Wrath*. His smaller 12" signature snare and Mapex Black Panther setup emphasized quick, responsive articulation for the band's syncopated riff-locking grooves. Since 2019, Art Cruz has carried that groove-metal intensity forward with his own powerful, precise style, integrating seamlessly into the band's sound on the 2020 self-titled album and *Omens* (2022) while using a Ludwig-based setup with an extensive Zildjian cymbal array for added texture.
