## Drummer(s)

**Daray** — 2006–2016
Dariusz "Daray" Brzozowski drummed for Vader, one of the longest-running and most internationally recognized death metal bands in Europe, for a decade. He recorded *Welcome to the Morbid Reich* (2011) and *Tibi et Igni* (2014) with the band, bringing extreme speed and technical precision honed through Polish black metal band Vesania. During part of his Vader tenure, Daray simultaneously drummed for Norwegian symphonic black metal act Dimmu Borgir, joining them in 2008 before leaving Vader in 2016 to focus exclusively on Dimmu Borgir.

Vader's drumming demands extreme speed and precision, and Daray delivered a decade of blistering blast beats and sustained double-bass work that anchored the band's assault on albums like *Welcome to the Morbid Reich* (2011).
