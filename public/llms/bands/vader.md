## Drummer(s)

**Daray** — 2006–2016
Dariusz "Daray" Brzozowski (born August 23, 1984, in Warsaw, Poland) is a Polish drummer renowned for his work with Norwegian symphonic black metal band Dimmu Borgir since 2008 and his 10-year tenure with Polish death metal legends Vader (2006–2016). Daray's drumming combines extreme speed and technical precision with theatrical flair, making him equally suited to Vader's blast-driven death metal assault and Dimmu Borgir's grandiose symphonic productions. His ability to perform complex, blistering patterns while maintaining tight dynamic control has established him as one of the premier drummers in extreme metal.
