## Biography

Jimmy DeGrasso (born March 16, 1963, in Pennsylvania) is an American heavy metal drummer best known for his 1998–2002 tenure with Megadeth, recording Risk (1999) and The World Needs a Hero (2001) and appearing on the band's first live album, Rude Awakening (2002). Before Megadeth, DeGrasso built a touring and session résumé spanning Ozzy Osbourne (The Ultimate Sin, 1986), Y&T (1987–1990), Suicidal Tendencies (1992–1995), and Alice Cooper (1995–1997), and he later drummed for Black Star Riders (2012–2017).
