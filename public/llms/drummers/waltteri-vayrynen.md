## Biography

Waltteri Väyrynen (born July 5, 1994, in Helsinki, Finland) is a Finnish drummer best known for his tenure in gothic/doom pioneers Paradise Lost, joining as a session player in 2015 before becoming an official member from 2016 to 2022 and recording Medusa (2017) and Obsidian (2020). In 2020 he also drummed for Bodom After Midnight — the project formed by former Children of Bodom members Alexi Laiho and Daniel Freyberg — appearing on the posthumous EP Paint the Sky with Blood (2021) before the band ended following Laiho's death that December. He has been an official member of Finnish death metal band Abhorrence since 2018, and in September 2022 joined Swedish progressive metal band Opeth, replacing Martin Axenrot and recording The Last Will and Testament (2024). He has also done session and touring work for Vallenfyre, Bloodbath, and Carcass.
