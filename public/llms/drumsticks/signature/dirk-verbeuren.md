# What Drumsticks Does Dirk Verbeuren Use? Tama O-DVM2

**Band:** Megadeth | **Brand:** Tama | **Model:** O-DVM2

## Direct Answer

Dirk Verbeuren plays the Tama O-DVM2 — Japanese Oak, Dirk Verbeuren Signature (216B basis) (0.63" diameter x 16" long), ball (precision-turned, wood) tip, not specified by manufacturer taper.
