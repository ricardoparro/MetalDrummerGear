# What Drumsticks Does Gavin Harrison Use? Vic Firth SHAR2

**Band:** Porcupine Tree / King Crimson | **Brand:** Vic Firth | **Model:** SHAR2

---

## Direct Answer

Gavin Harrison plays the Vic Firth SHAR2 — Hickory, royal blue matte grip area, Extended ROCK-style (longer/fatter than factory models) (0.63" dia. x 16.875" long), blended (wood) tip.
