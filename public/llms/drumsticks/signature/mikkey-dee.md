# What Drumsticks Does Mikkey Dee Use? Wincent Mikkey Dee Signature (W-MDS)

**Band:** Scorpions / Motörhead | **Brand:** Wincent | **Model:** Mikkey Dee Signature (W-MDS)

## Direct Answer

Mikkey Dee plays the Wincent Mikkey Dee Signature (W-MDS) — Kentucky hickory, UWP wax coating, 2B-style cut (0.622" diameter x 16.14" long), fat acorn (wood) tip, medium taper.
