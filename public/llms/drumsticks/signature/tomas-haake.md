# What Drumsticks Does Tomas Haake Use? Wincent WTHS

**Band:** Meshuggah | **Brand:** Wincent | **Model:** WTHS

---

## Direct Answer

Tomas Haake plays the Wincent WTHS — Hickory (stone-ground, wax-treated), Tomas Haake Signature (0.638" dia. x 16.38" long), bead (wood, enlarged) tip.
