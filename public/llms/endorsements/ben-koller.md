# Ben Koller — Brand Endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Ben Koller endorses Tama for drums. They play the Tama Starclassic Maple. This partnership began in 2001.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Ben Koller endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian HHX & AAX Series. This partnership began in 2001.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Ben Koller endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5B. This partnership began in 2001.

### Drumheads: Remo

Ben Koller endorses Remo for drumheads. This partnership began in 2001.

### Hardware / Pedals: Tama

Ben Koller endorses Tama for hardware / pedals. They play the Tama Iron Cobra 900 Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2001.

## Endorsement History

- **2001** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Converge's Jane Doe (2001) debuted the Tama kit and 14x6.5" brass snare formula that has anchored Koller's sound for two decades
- **2001** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — Jane Doe also introduced the Sabian HHX/AAX combination (14" HHX Groove Hats, HHX Raw Bell Dry Ride, AAX crashes) Koller has used on every Converge album since
- **2006** (Drums): Renewed Tama deal — No Heroes (2006) continued the same brass-shell Tama snare formula used since Jane Doe, now deployed with three albums' worth of studio confidence
