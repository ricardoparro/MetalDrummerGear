# Bill Ward — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Bill Ward
**Band:** Black Sabbath
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/bill-ward/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Ludwig

Bill Ward endorses Ludwig for drums. They play the Ludwig Standard / Club Date Series. This partnership began in 1968.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Bill Ward endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian Avedis Series. This partnership began in 1968.

### Drumsticks: Pro-Mark

Bill Ward endorses Pro-Mark for drumsticks. They play the Pro-Mark Standard 5A/5B equivalent. This partnership began in 1970.

### Drumheads: Remo

Bill Ward endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Ambassador Coated. This partnership began in 1970.

### Hardware / Pedals: Ludwig

Bill Ward endorses Ludwig for hardware / pedals. They play the Ludwig Speed King Pedal. This partnership began in 1970.

## Endorsement History

- **1968** (Drums): Signed with Ludwig — Played a Ludwig kit from Black Sabbath's formation in Birmingham through the "Paranoid" and "Master of Reality" sessions
- **1970** (Cymbals): Signed with Zildjian — Avedis Zildjian hi-hats, ride, and crash set the cymbal template heard on "Black Sabbath" and "Paranoid"
- **1971** (Drums): Renewed Ludwig deal — Upgraded to a 22" Ludwig bass drum (from the 20" used on "Paranoid") to match Tony Iommi's down-tuned riffs on "Master of Reality"

## FAQ
