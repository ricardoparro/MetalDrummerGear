# Bill Ward — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Bill Ward
**Band:** Black Sabbath
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/bill-ward/endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Ludwig

Bill Ward endorses Ludwig for drums. They play the Ludwig Classic Maple. This partnership began in 1968.

### Cymbals: Paiste

Bill Ward endorses Paiste for cymbals. They play the Paiste 2002 & Giant Beat Series. This partnership began in 1975.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Bill Ward endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 2B. This partnership began in 1970s.

### Drumheads: Remo

Bill Ward endorses Remo for drumheads. This partnership began in 1970s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Ludwig

Bill Ward endorses Ludwig for hardware / pedals. They play the Ludwig Atlas Pro Double Pedal. This partnership began in 1970s.

## Endorsement History

- **1970** (Drums): Signed with Ludwig — Black Sabbath's debut album introduced Ward's Ludwig Super Classic kit, anchored by a Ludwig Acrolite snare and Speed King pedal used throughout the classic Ozzy-era lineup
- **1970** (Cymbals): Signed with Zildjian — Avedis Zildjian hi-hats, ride, and crash carried Paranoid (1970) and Master of Reality (1971)
- **1975** (Cymbals): Switched from Zildjian to Paiste — Sabotage marked the switch to an expanded Paiste 2002 setup — 15" hi-hats, a 24" ride, and Ward's first-ever 18" China cymbal
