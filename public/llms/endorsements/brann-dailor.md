## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Brann Dailor endorses Tama for drums. They play the Tama Starclassic Performer B/B. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Brann Dailor endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Mb20 & Mb8 Series. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumsticks: Vater

Brann Dailor endorses Vater for drumsticks. They play the Vater 5B. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumheads: Evans

Brann Dailor endorses Evans for drumheads. They play the Evans G2 Clear. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Tama

Brann Dailor endorses Tama for hardware / pedals. They play the Tama Speed Cobra. This partnership began in 2010s.

## Endorsement History

- **2002** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Joined Tama family during Remission era
- **2004** (Cymbals): Signed with Meinl — Switched to Meinl for their dark, complex tones
