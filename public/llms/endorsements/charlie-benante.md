# Charlie Benante — Brand Endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Charlie Benante endorses Tama for drums. This partnership began in 1980s.

### Cymbals: Paiste

Charlie Benante endorses Paiste for cymbals. This partnership began in 1980s.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Charlie Benante endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Drumheads: Evans

Charlie Benante endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Tama

Charlie Benante endorses Tama for hardware / pedals. They play the Tama Speed Cobra. This partnership began in 2010s.

## Endorsement History

- **1985** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Pioneer of thrash drumming, early Tama endorser
- **2022** (Drums): Renewed Tama deal — Continued with Tama for Pantera reunion tours
