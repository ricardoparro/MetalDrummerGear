# Chris Adler — Brand Endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Mapex

Chris Adler endorses Mapex for drums. They play the Mapex Saturn in Satin Black Maple Burl. This partnership began in 2010s. Chris Adler has a co-designed signature product with Mapex.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Chris Adler endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Byzance & Pure Alloy. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumsticks: Promark

Chris Adler endorses Promark for drumsticks. They play the Promark TX5AXW Chris Adler Signature. This partnership began in 2000s. Chris Adler has a co-designed signature product with Promark.

### Drumheads: Evans

Chris Adler endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Trick

Chris Adler endorses Trick for hardware / pedals. They play the Trick Pro V. This partnership began in 2010s.

## Signature Models

- Mapex Saturn in Satin Black Maple Burl (signature)
- Promark TX5AXW Chris Adler Signature (signature)
- Mapex Warbird Signature Snare (2005)

## Endorsement History

- **2005** (Drums): Signature product: Mapex Warbird Signature Snare — Signature snare designed for aggressive attack
- **2008** (Drumsticks): Signature product: Promark TX5AXW Chris Adler Signature — Signature sticks with modified 5AX design
- **2019** (Drums): Ended Mapex endorsement — Left Lamb of God due to Musician's Dystonia, continued with Mapex for Firstborne
