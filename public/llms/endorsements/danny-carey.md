## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Sonor

Danny Carey endorses Sonor for drums. They play the Sonor Custom. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Cymbals: Paiste

Danny Carey endorses Paiste for cymbals. They play the Paiste Signature Series. This partnership began in 1990s. Danny Carey has a co-designed signature product with Paiste.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Danny Carey endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth Danny Carey Signature. This partnership began in 2000s. Danny Carey has a co-designed signature product with Vic Firth.

### Drumheads: Evans

Danny Carey endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Electronics: Mandala

Danny Carey endorses Mandala for electronics. This partnership began in 2000s.
