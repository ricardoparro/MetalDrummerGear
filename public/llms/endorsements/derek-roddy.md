# Derek Roddy — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Derek Roddy
**Band:** Nile (ex-Hate Eternal)
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/derek-roddy/endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: DW

Derek Roddy endorses DW for drums. They play the DW Collector's Series Maple. This partnership began in 2001.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Derek Roddy endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian HHX / AAX Series. This partnership began in 1994.

### Drumsticks: Vater

Derek Roddy endorses Vater for drumsticks. They play the Vater 5B. This partnership began in 2001.

### Drumheads: Evans

Derek Roddy endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2001.

### Hardware / Pedals: Axis

Derek Roddy endorses Axis for hardware / pedals. They play the Axis A Longboard Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2000.

## Endorsement History

- **1994** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — Began his career with Sabian cymbals in the underground death metal scene during his formative years with Aurora Borealis
- **2000** (Hardware / Pedals): Signed with Axis — Adopted Axis A Longboard double pedals during his stint as Nile's live drummer, the direct-drive longboard action his heel-toe technique relies on at extreme tempos
- **2001** (Drums): Signed with DW — Signed with DW Collector's Series drums at the start of his Hate Eternal tenure, the kit heard on King of All Kings (2002) and I, Monarch (2005)
- **2001** (Drumsticks): Signed with Vater — Settled on Vater 5B sticks for the balance of control and endurance his freehand/gravity blast beat technique requires
