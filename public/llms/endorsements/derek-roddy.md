# Derek Roddy — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Derek Roddy

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: DW

Derek Roddy endorses DW for drums. They play the DW Collector's Series Maple. This partnership began in 2001.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Derek Roddy endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian HHX / AAX Series. This partnership began in 2001.

### Drumsticks: Vater

Derek Roddy endorses Vater for drumsticks. They play the Vater 5B. This partnership began in 2001.

### Drumheads: Evans

Derek Roddy endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2001.

### Hardware / Pedals: Axis

Derek Roddy endorses Axis for hardware / pedals. They play the Axis A Longboard Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2001.

## Endorsement History

- **1994** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — Adopted Sabian cymbals during his formative Aurora Borealis years, developing the extreme-tempo technique that would define his career
- **2001** (Drums): Signed with DW — Built his DW Collector's Series Maple kit heading into Hate Eternal's King of All Kings sessions (2002)
- **2001** (Cymbals): Switched from Sabian to Sabian HHX/AAX — Refined to the HHX/AAX hybrid setup for the fast attack and controlled decay his gravity blast technique demands
- **2001** (Hardware / Pedals): Signed with Axis — Adopted the Axis A Longboard double pedal, whose direct-drive action and longboard footboard suit his heel-toe technique at extreme tempos
