# Flo Mounier — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Flo Mounier
**Band:** Cryptopsy
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/flo-mounier/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Flo Mounier endorses Tama for drums. They play the Tama Starclassic Maple. This partnership began in 2012.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Flo Mounier endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian AAX & HHX. This partnership began in 2012.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Flo Mounier endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5A. This partnership began in 1996.

### Drumheads: Evans

Flo Mounier endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 1996.

### Hardware / Pedals: Tama

Flo Mounier endorses Tama for hardware / pedals. They play the Tama Speed Cobra. This partnership began in 2012.

## Endorsement History

- **1996** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — Cryptopsy's genre-defining None So Vile (1996) documented Mounier's Pearl MX Series kit and Pearl Eliminator double pedal
- **1996** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — None So Vile also introduced Sabian AA cymbals, Mounier's first endorsed cymbal setup
- **2005** (Cymbals): Switched from Paiste to Zildjian — Once Was Not (2005) brought a switch to Zildjian ZXT cymbals and a new Yamaha Recording Custom kit, replacing the Paiste Alpha series used since Whisper Supremacy (1998)
- **2012** (Cymbals): Switched from Zildjian to Sabian — Mounier's modern Tama Starclassic Maple kit and return to Sabian AAX/HHX cymbals define his current setup, documented on Cryptopsy's 2012 self-titled album through 2023's As Gomorrah Burns

## FAQ
