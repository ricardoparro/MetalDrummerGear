# Gavin Harrison — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Gavin Harrison
**Band:** Porcupine Tree / King Crimson
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/gavin-harrison/endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Sonor

Gavin Harrison endorses Sonor for drums. They play the Sonor SQ2 Series. This partnership began in 2002.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Gavin Harrison endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian K Custom Special Dry. This partnership began in 2002.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Gavin Harrison endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth Gavin Harrison Signature. This partnership began in 2007. Gavin Harrison has a co-designed signature product with Vic Firth.

### Drumheads: Remo

Gavin Harrison endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Ambassador. This partnership began in 2002.

### Hardware / Pedals: Sonor

Gavin Harrison endorses Sonor for hardware / pedals. They play the Sonor Perfect Balance Pedal. This partnership began in 2007.

## Signature Models

- Vic Firth Gavin Harrison Signature (signature)
- Sonor Gavin Harrison Signature 14" x 5.25" Snare (2007)

## Endorsement History

- **2002** (Drums): Signed with Sonor — Joining Porcupine Tree for In Absentia (2002), Harrison began an evolving custom Sonor maple kit relationship that grew into the full SQ2 series
- **2002** (Cymbals): Signed with Zildjian — In Absentia debuted the Zildjian K Custom Special Dry cymbals — co-developed with Zildjian — that Harrison has used for his entire Porcupine Tree and King Crimson tenure
- **2007** (Drums): Signature product: Sonor Gavin Harrison Signature 14" x 5.25" Snare — Fear of a Blank Planet (2007) marked the fully realized Sonor Gavin Harrison Signature brass snare and Sonor Perfect Balance pedal, the product of years of collaboration with Sonor
