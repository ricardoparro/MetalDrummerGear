# Gavin Harrison — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Gavin Harrison
**Band:** Porcupine Tree / King Crimson
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/gavin-harrison/endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Sonor

Gavin Harrison endorses Sonor for drums. They play the Sonor SQ2 Series. This partnership began in 2002.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Gavin Harrison endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian K Custom Special Dry. This partnership began in 2007.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Gavin Harrison endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth Gavin Harrison Signature. This partnership began in 2007. Gavin Harrison has a co-designed signature product with Vic Firth.

### Drumheads: Remo

Gavin Harrison endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Ambassador Coated. This partnership began in 2002.

### Hardware / Pedals: Sonor

Gavin Harrison endorses Sonor for hardware / pedals. They play the Sonor Perfect Balance Pedal. This partnership began in 2002.

## Signature Models

- Vic Firth Gavin Harrison Signature (signature)
- Zildjian Zildjian K Custom Special Dry Series (2007)
- Sonor Sonor Gavin Harrison Signature Snares (14"x5.25" brass and 12" Protean) (2007)

## Endorsement History

- **2002** (Drums): Signed with Sonor — Brought a custom Sonor SQ2 shell pack to Porcupine Tree upon joining for "In Absentia," refined through "Deadwing"
- **2007** (Cymbals): Signature product: Zildjian Zildjian K Custom Special Dry Series — Co-developed the K Custom Special Dry cymbal line with Zildjian, in full use on "Fear of a Blank Planet"
- **2007** (Drums): Signature product: Sonor Sonor Gavin Harrison Signature Snares (14"x5.25" brass and 12" Protean) — Sonor released signature snare drums developed with Harrison for ghost-note sensitivity and rimshot projection
