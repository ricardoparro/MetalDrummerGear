## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

George Kollias endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Masterworks Series. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

George Kollias endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian A Custom Series. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

George Kollias endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumheads: Evans

George Kollias endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Pearl

George Kollias endorses Pearl for hardware / pedals. They play the Pearl Demon XR Pedals. This partnership began in 2010s. George Kollias has a co-designed signature product with Pearl.

## Signature Models

- Pearl Demon XR Pedals (signature)
- Pearl Demon XR Bass Drum Pedals (2015)
