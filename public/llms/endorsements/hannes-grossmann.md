# Hannes Grossmann — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Hannes Grossmann
**Band:** Alkaloid (ex-Obscura, ex-Necrophagist)

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: DW

Hannes Grossmann endorses DW for drums. They play the DW Collector's Series. This partnership began in 2014.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Hannes Grossmann endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Byzance Series. This partnership began in 2001.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Hannes Grossmann endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5B. This partnership began in 2014.

### Drumheads: Evans

Hannes Grossmann endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2014.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Hannes Grossmann endorses DW for hardware / pedals. They play the DW 9000 Series Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2014.

## Endorsement History

- **2001** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Built a Tama Starclassic Maple kit with a Tama S.L.P. G-Maple snare during his Necrophagist years (Epitaph, 2004)
- **2001** (Cymbals): Signed with Meinl — Established his Meinl Byzance cymbal setup, a relationship carried forward unchanged through Obscura and into Alkaloid
- **2007** (Hardware / Pedals): Signed with Tama — Paired his Starclassic kit with a Tama Iron Cobra double pedal through the Obscura breakthrough era (Cosmogenesis, 2009; Omnivium, 2011)
- **2014** (Drums): Switched from Tama Starclassic Maple to DW Collector's Series — Moved to a DW Collector's Series kit and 9000 Series double pedal upon founding Alkaloid, a setup carried through The Malkuth Grimoire (2015) and Liquid Anatomy (2018)
