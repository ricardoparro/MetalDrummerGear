# Hellhammer — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Hellhammer
**Band:** Mayhem
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/hellhammer/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Hellhammer endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Reference Series. This partnership began in 2004.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Hellhammer endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian A Custom. This partnership began in 2004.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Hellhammer endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5B. This partnership began in 1994.

### Drumheads: Remo

Hellhammer endorses Remo for drumheads. This partnership began in 1994.

### Hardware / Pedals: Pearl

Hellhammer endorses Pearl for hardware / pedals. They play the Pearl Demon Drive. This partnership began in 1994.

## Endorsement History

- **1994** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — The Grieghallen sessions for Mayhem's genre-defining De Mysteriis Dom Sathanas (1994) used a Pearl kit paired with a Ludwig Acrolite snare and Gibraltar hardware
- **1994** (Cymbals): Signed with Paiste — Paiste 2002 Sound Edge hi-hats and a RUDE China shaped the cold, cutting cymbal sound of De Mysteriis Dom Sathanas
- **2004** (Drums): Renewed Pearl deal — Chimera (2004) documented the flagship Pearl Reference Series configuration that remained Hellhammer's kit through Esoteric Warfare (2014) and Daemon (2019)
- **2004** (Cymbals): Switched from Paiste to Zildjian — The Pearl Reference era brought a parallel switch to Zildjian A Custom cymbals, explicitly confirmed as Hellhammer's setup as of Daemon (2019)

## FAQ
