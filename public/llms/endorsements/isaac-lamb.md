# Isaac Lamb — Brand Endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: SJC Custom Drums

Isaac Lamb endorses SJC Custom Drums for drums. They play the SJC Custom Drums Custom Maple. This partnership began in 2009.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Isaac Lamb endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Classics Custom Dark Series. This partnership began in 2009.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Isaac Lamb endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 2B. This partnership began in 2009.

### Drumheads: Evans

Isaac Lamb endorses Evans for drumheads. They play the Evans Hydraulic. This partnership began in 2009.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Isaac Lamb endorses DW for hardware / pedals. They play the DW 9000 Series Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2009.

## Endorsement History

- **2009** (Drums): Signed with SJC Custom Drums — Built his hand-built SJC Custom maple kit while founding the band in Sherman, Texas, a setup carried unchanged through the band's self-titled 2012 debut
- **2009** (Cymbals): Signed with Meinl — Adopted the Meinl Classics Custom Dark series for its trashy, low-pitched voicing across Balancing Survival and Happiness (2014) and Nomad (2017)
- **2009** (Hardware / Pedals): Signed with DW — Paired his SJC kit with a DW 9000 Series double pedal, a combination maintained through Absolute (2019)
- **2022** (Drums): Renewed undefined deal — Maintained his SJC Custom setup into Lowest Form of Animal (2022), the same rig documented across the entire Battlecross catalog
