# Jaska Raatikainen — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Jaska Raatikainen
**Band:** Children of Bodom
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/jaska-raatikainen/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Jaska Raatikainen endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Masters Premium Maple. This partnership began in 2005.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Jaska Raatikainen endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian A Custom & K Custom Series. This partnership began in 1999.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Jaska Raatikainen endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5A. This partnership began in 1999.

### Drumheads: Remo

Jaska Raatikainen endorses Remo for drumheads. This partnership began in 2005.

### Hardware / Pedals: Pearl

Jaska Raatikainen endorses Pearl for hardware / pedals. They play the Pearl Eliminator Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2005.

## Endorsement History

- **1999** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Moved to an early Tama custom maple kit for Hatebreeder (1999), Children of Bodom's first endorsed gear setup after the pre-endorsement Pearl kit used on debut Something Wild (1997)
- **1999** (Cymbals): Signed with Zildjian — Zildjian A Series cymbals became an official partnership on Hatebreeder (1999), continuing the brand Raatikainen had played since Something Wild
- **2005** (Drums): Switched from Tama to Pearl — Are You Dead Yet? (2005) marked the switch to Pearl Masters Premium Maple — the kit he played through Children of Bodom's final album Hexed (2019)
- **2005** (Hardware / Pedals): Switched from DW to Pearl — The Pearl Eliminator double pedal replaced the DW 5000 used since Hatebreeder, debuting alongside the Pearl Masters kit on Are You Dead Yet?
