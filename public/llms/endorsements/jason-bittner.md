# Jason Bittner — Brand Endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Jason Bittner endorses Tama for drums. They play the Tama Starclassic Performer B/B. This partnership began in 2002.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Jason Bittner endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian HHX / HH Series. This partnership began in 1997.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Jason Bittner endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth 5B. This partnership began in 2002.

### Drumheads: Remo

Jason Bittner endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Powerstroke 3 / Ambassador Coated. This partnership began in 2002.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Jason Bittner endorses DW for hardware / pedals. They play the DW 9002 Double Bass Pedal. This partnership began in 2002.

## Endorsement History

- **1997** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — Began his Sabian relationship during early Shadows Fall records (Somber Eyes to the Sky, 1997; Of One Blood, 2000)
- **2002** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Established the Tama Starclassic Performer B/B kit that would define the Shadows Fall era, debuting on The Art of Balance (2002)
- **2002** (Hardware / Pedals): Signed with DW — Adopted the DW 9002 dual-chain double bass pedal alongside his Starclassic kit for The Art of Balance (2002)
- **2012** (Drums): Renewed undefined deal — Carried the identical Tama/Sabian/DW rig into his Overkill tenure starting with The Electric Age (2012), running both bands on the same setup
