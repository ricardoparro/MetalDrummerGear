## Current Endorsements

### Drums: SJC Custom Drums

Jay Weinberg endorses SJC Custom Drums for drums. This partnership began in 2014.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Jay Weinberg endorses Zildjian for cymbals. This partnership began in 2014.

### Drumsticks: Vater

Jay Weinberg endorses Vater for drumsticks. They play the Vater 5B. This partnership began in 2014.

### Drumheads: Evans

Jay Weinberg endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2014.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Jay Weinberg endorses DW for hardware / pedals. This partnership began in 2014.

## Endorsement History

- **2014** (Drums): Signed with SJC Custom Drums — Joined SJC when becoming Slipknot drummer
- **2023** (Drums): Ended SJC endorsement — Left Slipknot in November 2023, maintained all endorsements
- **2024** (Drums): Renewed SJC Custom Drums deal — Continued with SJC after joining Suicidal Tendencies

## FAQ
