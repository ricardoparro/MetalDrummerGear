# Jocke Wallgren — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Jocke Wallgren
**Band:** Amon Amarth

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Jocke Wallgren endorses Tama for drums. They play the Tama Starclassic Performer B/B. This partnership began in 2013.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Jocke Wallgren endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Byzance Traditional. This partnership began in 2013.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Jocke Wallgren endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth 5B. This partnership began in 2013.

### Drumheads: Remo

Jocke Wallgren endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Powerstroke 3 / Emperor Coated. This partnership began in 2013.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Jocke Wallgren endorses DW for hardware / pedals. They play the DW 9002 Double Bass Pedal. This partnership began in 2013.

## Endorsement History

- **2005** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Developed his Tama kit and early Meinl Byzance cymbal setup during his years with Evergrey and Rage, ahead of joining Amon Amarth
- **2013** (Drums): Signed with Tama Starclassic Performer B/B — Fully configured his Starclassic Performer B/B kit and DW 9002 pedal upon replacing Fredrik Andersson for Deceiver of the Gods (2013)
- **2019** (Cymbals): Switched from Meinl Byzance Traditional to Meinl Byzance Vintage — Switched to the drier, trashier Byzance Vintage sub-line for Berserker (2019) to suit its blast-beat sections
- **2022** (Cymbals): Switched from Meinl Byzance Vintage to Meinl Byzance Traditional — Reverted to the standard Byzance Traditional series for The Great Heathen Army (2022)
