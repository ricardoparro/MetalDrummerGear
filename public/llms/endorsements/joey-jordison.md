# Joey Jordison — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Joey Jordison
**Band:** Slipknot

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Joey Jordison endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Reference Series. This partnership began in 2010.

### Cymbals: Paiste

Joey Jordison endorses Paiste for cymbals. They play the Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series. This partnership began in 2000.

### Drumsticks: Promark

Joey Jordison endorses Promark for drumsticks. They play the Promark TX515W Joey Jordison. This partnership began in 2008. Joey Jordison has a co-designed signature product with Promark.

### Drumheads: Evans

Joey Jordison endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2005.

### Hardware / Pedals: Pearl

Joey Jordison endorses Pearl for hardware / pedals. They play the Pearl Demon Drive. This partnership began in 2010.

## Signature Models

- Promark TX515W Joey Jordison (signature)
- Pearl Joey Jordison Signature Snare 13x6.5" (2010)

## Endorsement History

- **1999** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — Signed with Pearl after Slipknot debut album success
- **1999** (Cymbals): Switched from Zildjian to Paiste — Switched to Paiste RUDE series for heavier, more aggressive sound
- **2008** (Drumsticks): Switched from Ahead to Promark — Moved from Ahead aluminum sticks to Promark signature wood sticks
- **2010** (Drums): Signature product: Pearl Joey Jordison Signature Snare 13x6.5" — Signature snare with unique blood splatter finish
