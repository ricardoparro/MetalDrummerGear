# John Otto — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** John Otto
**Band:** Limp Bizkit
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/john-otto/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: OCDP

John Otto endorses OCDP for drums. They play the OCDP Custom Type 5 Acrylic. This partnership began in 2003.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

John Otto endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian A & A Custom Series. This partnership began in 1997.

### Drumsticks: Zildjian

John Otto endorses Zildjian for drumsticks. They play the Zildjian Artist Series. This partnership began in 1997.

### Drumheads: Remo

John Otto endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Emperor Coated. This partnership began in 1997.

### Hardware / Pedals: Gibraltar

John Otto endorses Gibraltar for hardware / pedals. They play the Gibraltar G Class Bass Drum Pedals. This partnership began in 2003.

## Endorsement History

- **1997** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — Limp Bizkit's debut Three Dollar Bill, Y'all$ (1997) documented Otto's Pearl Export kit
- **1997** (Cymbals): Signed with Zildjian — Zildjian A and A Custom cymbals have anchored Otto's kit since Limp Bizkit's 1997 debut
- **1999** (Drums): Switched from Pearl to Sonor — Significant Other (1999) saw Otto move to a Sonor Force 3007 kit with a 14x6.5" steel snare
- **2003** (Drums): Switched from Sonor to OCDP — Results May Vary (2003) introduced the custom Orange County Drum & Percussion maple-shell kit Otto has played ever since, through Gold Cobra (2011) and Still Sucks (2021)

## FAQ

**Q: What brands does John Otto endorse?**
A: John Otto endorses OCDP, Zildjian, Remo, Gibraltar. Their primary drum endorsement is OCDP and they play Zildjian cymbals.

**Q: Does John Otto have a signature drum or cymbal?**
A: John Otto is a key OCDP endorser but does not have a dedicated signature kit model in the current lineup.
