# Matt Greiner — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Matt Greiner

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Greiner & Kilmer

Matt Greiner endorses Greiner & Kilmer for drums. They play the Greiner & Kilmer Custom. This partnership began in 2012.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Matt Greiner endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Byzance Series. This partnership began in 2015.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Matt Greiner endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5A. This partnership began in 2005.

### Drumheads: Evans

Matt Greiner endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2005.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Matt Greiner endorses DW for hardware / pedals. They play the DW 9000 Series Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2007.

## Signature Models

- Greiner & Kilmer Greiner & Kilmer Custom (2012)

## Endorsement History

- **2005** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — August Burns Red's debut Thrill Seeker (2005) documented Greiner's Sabian B8 series cymbals
- **2007** (Drums): Signed with Mapex — Messengers (2007) introduced the Mapex Saturn IV hybrid maple/walnut kit that carried ABR's commercial breakthrough through Constellations (2009)
- **2011** (Cymbals): Switched from Zildjian to Paiste — Leveler (2011) paired a Ludwig Classic Maple kit with a switch to Paiste 2002 cymbals for ABR's Grammy-nominated record
- **2012** (Drums): Signature product: Greiner & Kilmer Greiner & Kilmer Custom — Greiner co-founded the custom drum company Greiner & Kilmer with fellow drummer Kaleb Kilmer, developing the custom kit he plays today
