# Matt Greiner — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Matt Greiner
**Band:** August Burns Red
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/matt-greiner/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Matt Greiner endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Reference Pure. This partnership began in 2011.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Matt Greiner endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Byzance Series. This partnership began in 2011.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Matt Greiner endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth Matt Greiner Signature. This partnership began in 2017. Matt Greiner has a co-designed signature product with Vic Firth.

### Drumheads: Remo

Matt Greiner endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Controlled Sound Coated. This partnership began in 2003.

### Hardware / Pedals: Pearl

Matt Greiner endorses Pearl for hardware / pedals. They play the Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal. This partnership began in 2011.

## Signature Models

- Vic Firth Matt Greiner Signature (signature)
- Vic Firth Matt Greiner Signature Drumsticks (2017)
- Pearl Matt Greiner Signature Snare (2017)

## Endorsement History

- **2003** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — Co-founded August Burns Red playing Pearl Export/Vision kits, with a developing Meinl cymbal endorsement
- **2011** (Drums): Renewed Pearl deal — Upgraded to Pearl Reference kits and solidified the Meinl Byzance cymbal setup around "Leveler" and "Found in Far Away Places"
- **2017** (Drumsticks): Signature product: Vic Firth Matt Greiner Signature Drumsticks — Received a Vic Firth signature stick model with a custom taper and bead
- **2017** (Drums): Signature product: Pearl Matt Greiner Signature Snare — Pearl released a Matt Greiner signature snare drum, part of the Reference Pure era setup
