## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Matt Halpern endorses Pearl for drums. This partnership began in 2015. Matt Halpern has a co-designed signature product with Pearl.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Matt Halpern endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Artist Concept. This partnership began in 2010s. Matt Halpern has a co-designed signature product with Meinl.

### Drumsticks: Promark

Matt Halpern endorses Promark for drumsticks. This partnership began in 2015. Matt Halpern has a co-designed signature product with Promark.

### Drumheads: Evans

Matt Halpern endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2010s.
