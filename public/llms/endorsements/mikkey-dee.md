# Mikkey Dee — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Mikkey Dee
**Band:** Scorpions / Motörhead

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Yamaha

Mikkey Dee endorses Yamaha for drums. They play the Yamaha Recording Custom. This partnership began in 1999.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Mikkey Dee endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian A Custom & K Series. This partnership began in 1999.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Mikkey Dee endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth Mikkey Dee Signature. This partnership began in 1999. Mikkey Dee has a co-designed signature product with Vic Firth.

### Drumheads: Remo

Mikkey Dee endorses Remo for drumheads. This partnership began in 1999.

### Hardware / Pedals: Yamaha

Mikkey Dee endorses Yamaha for hardware / pedals. They play the Yamaha FP9 Double Pedal. This partnership began in 1999.

## Signature Models

- Vic Firth Mikkey Dee Signature (signature)

## Endorsement History

- **1992** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Joining Motörhead in 1992, Dee brought a Tama Artstar II/Swingstar kit documented on his debut Bastards (1993)
- **1992** (Cymbals): Signed with Paiste — Paiste Giant Beat hi-hats and 2002 series cymbals powered Dee's early Motörhead era on Bastards (1993)
- **1999** (Drums): Switched from Tama to Yamaha — Dee transitioned to a Yamaha Recording Custom kit in the late 1990s, audible from We Are Motörhead (2000) through Inferno (2004)
- **1999** (Cymbals): Switched from Paiste to Zildjian — The Yamaha switch came paired with a move to Zildjian A Custom and K Series cymbals — the combination Dee still plays with Scorpions today
