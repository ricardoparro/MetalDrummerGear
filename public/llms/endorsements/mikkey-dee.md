# Mikkey Dee — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Mikkey Dee
**Band:** Scorpions / Motörhead
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/mikkey-dee/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Yamaha

Mikkey Dee endorses Yamaha for drums. They play the Yamaha Recording Custom / Oak Custom. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Cymbals: Zildjian

Mikkey Dee endorses Zildjian for cymbals. They play the Zildjian A Custom & K Custom Series. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Mikkey Dee endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth 2B / American Classic 5B. This partnership began in 1985.

### Drumheads: Remo

Mikkey Dee endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Emperor Coated / Powerstroke 3. This partnership began in 1992.

### Hardware / Pedals: Yamaha

Mikkey Dee endorses Yamaha for hardware / pedals. They play the Yamaha FP9 Flying Dragon Pedal. This partnership began in 1990s.

## Endorsement History

- **1992** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Joined Motörhead playing a Tama Artstar II/Swingstar kit with Paiste cymbals, first heard on "March ör Die" and "Bastards"
- **1999** (Drums): Switched from Tama to Yamaha — Gradually transitioned to Yamaha Recording Custom drums, audible from "We Are Motörhead" (2000) and "Inferno" (2004) onward
- **1999** (Cymbals): Switched from Paiste to Zildjian — Cymbal setup moved from Paiste to Zildjian alongside the Yamaha drum transition
- **2016** (Drums): Renewed Yamaha deal — Carried the Yamaha/Zildjian setup into Scorpions after joining the band in 2016 following Lemmy Kilmister's death
