# Paul Mazurkiewicz — Brand Endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Paul Mazurkiewicz endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Reference. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Cymbals: Meinl

Paul Mazurkiewicz endorses Meinl for cymbals. They play the Meinl Classics Custom / Byzance Series. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Paul Mazurkiewicz endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth Paul Mazurkiewicz Signature. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumheads: Remo

Paul Mazurkiewicz endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Powerstroke 3 / Emperor Coated. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Pearl

Paul Mazurkiewicz endorses Pearl for hardware / pedals. They play the Pearl Eliminator Double Bass Pedal. This partnership began in 1990s.

## Signature Models

- Vic Firth Paul Mazurkiewicz Signature (2000)

## Endorsement History

- **1990** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — Played a pre-endorsement Pearl Export-range kit on Cannibal Corpse's debut "Eaten Back to Life" (1990), recorded at Morrisound with producer Scott Burns
- **1990** (Cymbals): Signed with Meinl — Moved on from the budget Paiste/Zildjian cymbals used on "Eaten Back to Life" as the Meinl endorsement developed through the early Morrisound era
- **1996** (Drums): Switched from Pearl Export to Pearl Reference — Solidified the Pearl Reference maple/African mahogany hybrid shell pack as his primary kit, paired with a Pearl Free-Floating steel snare
- **2000** (Drumsticks): Signature product: Vic Firth Paul Mazurkiewicz Signature — Developed a Vic Firth signature stick spec — heavier overall weight and a durable wood tip built for the power demands of 180-250 BPM blast beats
