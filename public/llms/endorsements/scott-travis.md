# Scott Travis — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Scott Travis
**Band:** Judas Priest
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/scott-travis/endorsements

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Tama

Scott Travis endorses Tama for drums. They play the Tama Starclassic Maple. This partnership began in 2018.

### Cymbals: Paiste

Scott Travis endorses Paiste for cymbals. They play the Paiste Signature / 2002 Series. This partnership began in 1987.

### Drumsticks: Vater

Scott Travis endorses Vater for drumsticks. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumheads: Remo

Scott Travis endorses Remo for drumheads. This partnership began in 1990.

### Hardware / Pedals: Tama

Scott Travis endorses Tama for hardware / pedals. They play the Tama Iron Cobra / Speed Cobra Pedals. This partnership began in 1990.

## Endorsement History

- **1987** (Cymbals): Signed with Paiste — Established his Paiste endorsement during his Racer X years, before joining Judas Priest
- **1990** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Debuted on Tama Artstar II kit for "Painkiller," one of metal's most influential drumming performances
- **2005** (Drums): Switched from Tama to Pearl — Gradual migration to Pearl's Reference Series through the 2000s
- **2018** (Drums): Switched from Pearl to Tama — Returned to Tama's maple-shelled Starclassic line for "Firepower" and 2024's "Invincible Shield"

## FAQ
