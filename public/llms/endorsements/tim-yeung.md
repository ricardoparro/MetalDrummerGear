# Tim Yeung — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Tim Yeung
**Band:** Morbid Angel
**Profile:** https://metalforge.io/drummers/tim-yeung/endorsements

---

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Pearl

Tim Yeung endorses Pearl for drums. They play the Pearl Reference Masters. This partnership began in 2005.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Tim Yeung endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian AAX / HHX Series. This partnership began in 2005.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Tim Yeung endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth 5B. This partnership began in 2005.

### Drumheads: Remo

Tim Yeung endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Powerstroke 3. This partnership began in 2005.

### Hardware / Pedals: DW

Tim Yeung endorses DW for hardware / pedals. They play the DW 9002 Double Bass Pedal. This partnership began in 2005.

## Endorsement History

- **2005** (Drums): Signed with Pearl — Established his Pearl Reference Masters maple/mahogany kit with Erik Rutan's Hate Eternal on "I, Monarch" (2005), the setup he later carried unchanged into Divine Heresy and Morbid Angel
- **2005** (Cymbals): Signed with Sabian — Paired the Pearl kit with Sabian AAX/HHX cymbals starting on Hate Eternal's "I, Monarch" (2005), the same combination he used on Divine Heresy's "Bleed the Fifth" (2007) and Morbid Angel's "Illud Divinum Insanus" (2011)
- **2011** (Drums): Renewed Pearl deal — Carried the Pearl Reference Masters / Sabian / DW 9002 setup into his debut Morbid Angel album "Illud Divinum Insanus" (2011), maintaining it through "Kingdoms Disdained" (2017)
