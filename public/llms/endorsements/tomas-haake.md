## Current Endorsements

### Drums: Sonor

Tomas Haake endorses Sonor for drums. They play the Sonor SQ2 Series. This partnership began in 2005.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Tomas Haake endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian HHX & AAX Series. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Drumsticks: Wincent

Tomas Haake endorses Wincent for drumsticks. They play the Wincent Tomas Haake Signature. This partnership began in 2010. Tomas Haake has a co-designed signature product with Wincent.

### Drumheads: Remo

Tomas Haake endorses Remo for drumheads. They play the Remo Coated Emperor. This partnership began in 2000s.

### Hardware / Pedals: Tama

Tomas Haake endorses Tama for hardware / pedals. They play the Tama Speed Cobra. This partnership began in 2010s.

## Signature Models
