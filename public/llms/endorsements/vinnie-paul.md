# Vinnie Paul — Brand Endorsements

**Drummer:** Vinnie Paul

## Current Endorsements

### Drums: ddrum

Vinnie Paul endorses ddrum for drums. They play the ddrum Vinnie Paul Signature Series. This partnership began in 2008.

### Cymbals: Sabian

Vinnie Paul endorses Sabian for cymbals. They play the Sabian AA / AAX Series. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Drumsticks: Vic Firth

Vinnie Paul endorses Vic Firth for drumsticks. They play the Vic Firth American Classic 5B. This partnership began in 1990s.

### Drumheads: Evans

Vinnie Paul endorses Evans for drumheads. This partnership began in 2008.

### Hardware / Pedals: ddrum

Vinnie Paul endorses ddrum for hardware / pedals. This partnership began in 2008.

## Endorsement History

- **1990** (Drums): Signed with Tama — Played Tama drums during Pantera's major-label breakthrough on "Cowboys from Hell"
- **1996** (Drums): Switched from Tama to Pearl — Moved to Pearl drums, spanning "The Great Southern Trendkill" and "Reinventing the Steel"
- **2008** (Drums): Signed with ddrum — Signed with ddrum, who developed his Vinnie Paul Signature Series kit and snare for Hellyeah
