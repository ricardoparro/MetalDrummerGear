# Eloy Casagrande Drum Kit Evolution — Complete Timeline

Eloy Casagrande's career arc is the most compelling in modern metal drumming: a Brazilian teenager who joined Sepultura at 16, spent a decade building one of the most technically accomplished and distinctive setups in heavy music, and then landed the most high-profile drum role available — replacing Jay Weinberg in Slipknot in 2023. His gear evolution documents a journey from his first Pearl kit in São Paulo to the stadium-scale rig he now plays on Knotfest stages worldwide.

## Sepultura Debut Era (2011–2016)

**Albums:** The Mediator Between Head and Hands Must Also Be the Conductor (2013)
**Tours:** The Mediator World Tour 2013–2014; South American and European dates

Eloy Casagrande joined Sepultura in 2011 at just 16 years old — one of the most remarkable early-career signings in metal history. A teenager stepping into a band with a 25-year legacy and a catalog including Chaos A.D. and Roots demanded immediate professional-grade performance. His Pearl endorsement began with the band, giving him the reliable foundation needed for his first years of sustained international touring.

- **Drums:** Pearl Masters Premium (maple shells) — 22" kick, 10"/12"/14"/16" toms
- **Snare:** Pearl Free-Floating Steel 14"×6.5" (sharp, explosive attack for Sepultura's thrash/groove metal material)
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Cymbals — 14" hi-hats, 16"/18" crashes, 20" ride, 18" China
- **Hardware:** Pearl Eliminator Double Pedal (adjustable cam for controlled aggression)
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth 5B hickory (standard, early endorsement period)
- **Heads:** Remo Emperor double-ply on toms (durability for intensive touring as a teenage professional)
- **Estimated kit cost (original):** ~$8,000

**Key developments:**
- Joined Sepultura at age 16 (2011) — one of metal's most remarkable early signings
- Pearl Masters Premium endorsement begins immediately with the band
- Meinl cymbal endorsement established from early in his Sepultura tenure
- The Mediator Between Head and Hands (2013) announces his arrival as a world-class metal drummer

> "Being in Sepultura at 16 was a dream. I had to grow up fast — the music and the touring demanded everything from me immediately." — *Modern Drummer, 2014*

## Machine Messiah / Quadra Era (2017–2022)

**Albums:** Machine Messiah (2017), Quadra (2020), SepulQuarta (2021)
**Tours:** Machine Messiah World Tour; Quadra World Tour 2020–2022

The era where Eloy Casagrande fully came into his own as a defining drummer of the modern metal generation. Machine Messiah (2017) showcased a dramatically more sophisticated musical palette — combining Sepultura's Brazilian rhythmic identity with progressive metal structures. Quadra (2020) — a conceptual double album exploring Jungian psychology — is considered the high-water mark of his Sepultura work: technically demanding, dynamically varied, and musically ambitious. His setup expanded significantly to match these compositional ambitions.

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series (maple/birch hybrid) — 22" kick, 8"/10"/12"/14"/16" toms *(upgrade — broader tonal palette for Quadra's varied dynamics from acoustic passages to full brutality)*
- **Snare:** Pearl Free-Floating Steel 14"×6.5" *(continued — consistent snare choice across entire Sepultura tenure)*
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Classics Custom / Pure Alloy — 14" hi-hats, 16"/18"/19" crashes, 21" ride, 18" China *(upgrade — expanded setup for Quadra's dynamic range)*
- **Hardware:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal *(switch — direct drive for precise response at Machine Messiah's technical demands)*
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth 5A / Custom hickory sticks
- **Heads:** Remo Emperor on toms, EMAD on kick *(upgrade — EMAD for focused, punchy low-end on heavier material)*
- **Electronics:** Roland SPD-SX Sampling Pad *(new — electronics integrated for Quadra's layered studio arrangements in the live context)*
- **Estimated kit cost (original):** ~$14,000

**Key developments:**
- Upgraded to Pearl Reference — maple/birch hybrid for broader tonal range on Quadra
- Pearl Demon Drive direct drive double pedal adopted for Machine Messiah's precision demands
- Remo EMAD kick heads introduced for more focused low-end punch
- Roland SPD-SX integrated for live electronics during Quadra touring
- Quadra (2020) widely recognised as the defining achievement of his Sepultura career

> "Quadra gave me the chance to show every side of what I can do. Aggressive, melodic, dynamic — I had to be all of it." — *Revolver Magazine, 2020*

## Slipknot Era (2023–Present)

**Albums:** Upcoming Slipknot studio material
**Tours:** Slipknot World Tour 2024; Knotfest worldwide

The most dramatic transition in recent metal drumming history. In late 2023, Eloy Casagrande joined Slipknot — replacing Jay Weinberg and stepping into one of the most high-profile drum roles in all of heavy music. The move from Sepultura's underground credibility to Slipknot's stadium-scale productions represents a seismic shift: his current setup reflects the demands of performing for crowds of tens of thousands while maintaining the technical precision he built across his Sepultura decade.

- **Drums:** Pearl Masters Maple Complete — 22" kick, 8"/10"/12"/14"/16" toms *(upgrade — premium maple shells for studio-quality sound at stadium level)*
- **Snare:** Pearl Free-Floating Steel 14"×6.5" *(continued — the snare that has followed Eloy from Sepultura to Slipknot)*
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Cymbals (expanded configuration) — 14" hi-hats, multiple crashes 16"–19", 21" ride, China, splash cymbals *(upgrade — expanded to cover the full dynamic range of Slipknot's catalog)*
- **Hardware:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal *(continued — absolute reliability required for Slipknot's production scale)*
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth Eloy Casagrande Signature *(signature — custom hickory model for stadium performance)*
- **Heads:** Remo Emperor on toms, EMAD2 on kick *(upgrade — EMAD2 for tighter, more defined low-end in Slipknot's dense arena mix)*
- **Electronics:** Roland TD module + SPD-SX *(upgrade — full hybrid setup for Slipknot's layered production and sample-heavy live show)*
- **Estimated kit cost (original):** ~$25,000

**Key developments:**
- Joined Slipknot (2023) — replaced Jay Weinberg in one of metal's most iconic drum roles
- Vic Firth Eloy Casagrande signature sticks launched coinciding with the Slipknot chapter
- Expanded Meinl cymbal configuration for Slipknot's catalog breadth
- Remo EMAD2 kick heads for tighter arena-optimised low-end
- Roland TD module + SPD-SX for Slipknot's full hybrid production requirements
- Stadium-scale debut at Knotfest events worldwide

> "Slipknot is something else entirely. The stage, the production, the catalog — I had to absorb everything and make it my own from day one." — *Metal Hammer Interview, 2024*

## FAQ

**Q: What drum kit does Eloy Casagrande use with Slipknot?**
A: Eloy Casagrande plays a Pearl Masters Maple Complete kit with Slipknot — premium all-maple shells including a 22" kick drum and 8", 10", 12", 14", and 16" toms. He continues his long-standing Pearl endorsement from his Sepultura years, with the Masters Maple Complete representing an upgrade to the premium level required for Slipknot's stadium productions.

**Q: When did Eloy Casagrande join Slipknot?**
A: Eloy Casagrande joined Slipknot in 2023, replacing Jay Weinberg as the band's drummer. He made his first live appearances with Slipknot on their 2024 world tour and at Knotfest events, becoming the band's first non-American drummer in a core lineup role.

**Q: What kit did Eloy Casagrande use with Sepultura?**
A: During his Sepultura tenure (2011–2022), Eloy progressed through two Pearl kit generations: the Pearl Masters Premium (2011–2016) and the Pearl Reference Series (2017–2022). He used a Pearl Free-Floating Steel snare and Meinl cymbals throughout, with the Pearl Demon Drive double pedal from 2017 onwards.

**Q: What cymbals does Eloy Casagrande use?**
A: Eloy Casagrande uses Meinl cymbals throughout his career — from his Sepultura debut through to his Slipknot era. His current Slipknot setup includes an expanded Meinl configuration covering multiple crashes, hi-hats, a 21" ride, China, and splash cymbals to cover the full dynamic range of the band's catalog.

**Q: How old was Eloy Casagrande when he joined Sepultura?**
A: Eloy Casagrande was born on July 27, 1994, and joined Sepultura in 2011 — making him approximately 16 or 17 years old at the time. His debut album with the band, The Mediator Between Head and Hands Must Also Be the Conductor, was released in 2013.

**Q: What drumsticks does Eloy Casagrande use?**
A: Eloy Casagrande plays Vic Firth Eloy Casagrande Signature drumsticks — a custom hickory model launched around the time of his Slipknot debut in 2023. He previously used Vic Firth 5B and 5A sticks during his Sepultura tenure before the signature model was developed.

**Q: What makes Eloy Casagrande the standout modern metal drummer?**
A: Eloy Casagrande's story combines technical excellence with a narrative arc that has no peer in contemporary metal: Brazilian teenager → decade-long artistic development with Sepultura → replacing the successor to Joey Jordison in Slipknot. His Quadra work with Sepultura (2020) demonstrated compositional maturity beyond his years, and his Slipknot transition at age 28 represents one of the most dramatic career pivots in heavy music history.
