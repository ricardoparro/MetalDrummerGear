## Why Metal Drummers Choose the DW Collector's Maple

The DW Collector's Series is built with FinishPly technology, where a thin finish ply is applied to the inside of the shell, allowing full lacquer finishes without traditional paint that would dampen resonance. The result is a kit that looks stunning and sounds completely open. For metal drummers, this translates to maximum shell resonance and a brighter, more articulate sound compared to painted shells.

DW's proprietary bearing edge cutting produces a consistent bearing edge angle across all drums in the kit, ensuring uniform tone from drum to drum — a critical factor when playing in complex time signatures where every tom hit must speak clearly through a dense mix. The Collector's Maple's 7-ply maple construction delivers both the warmth metal drummers value and the projection needed on large stages.
