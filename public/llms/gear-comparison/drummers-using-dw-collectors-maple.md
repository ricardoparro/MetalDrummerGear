## Why Pro Metal Drummers Choose the DW Collector's Maple

The DW Collector's Maple is hand-built to order in DW's Oxnard, California factory, making it one of the most customizable production drum kits available. Key reasons professional metal drummers rely on it:

- **North American hard rock maple**: delivers a warm, full tone with clear high-end articulation at recording and live volumes
- **True-Pitch tension rods**: allow consistent tuning across the entire head, critical for even sustain in complex metal arrangements
- **Turret Lugs**: DW's signature lug design minimizes shell contact and improves resonance
- **Custom finish and sizing**: the Collector's line offers hundreds of finish options and shell size combinations, enabling personalized kit configurations for live and studio use
- **Handcrafted production**: every kit is built to specification in the USA, ensuring consistent quality across shell batches
