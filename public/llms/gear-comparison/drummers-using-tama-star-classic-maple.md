## Why Metal Drummers Choose the Tama Star Classic Maple

The Star Classic Maple's six-ply shell construction provides a balance between warmth and attack. Maple naturally produces a full fundamental tone with extended sustain — critical for getting a massive drum sound in a live mix. The Tama Star-Cast mounting system eliminates metal contact points between the shell and hardware, allowing the drum to resonate freely and produce a rounder, more open tone than traditional lug designs.

For metal drumming specifically, the Star Classic Maple handles heavy stick attacks without the shell "choking" or losing articulation at high volumes. Both Lars Ulrich and Jay Weinberg rely on this characteristic during high-intensity live performances where every hit needs to cut through loud stage volumes and dense guitar mixes.
