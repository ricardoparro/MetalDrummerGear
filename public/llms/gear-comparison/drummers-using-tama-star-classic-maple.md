### Jay Weinberg — Slipknot

Jay Weinberg joined Slipknot in 2014 following Joey Jordison's departure and immediately became known for his aggressive, precise drumming on the band's arena-filling tours. During his Slipknot tenure (2014–2023), Weinberg used the Tama Star Classic Maple as part of his live stage rig, pairing it with his signature SJC snare drum and Zildjian cymbals. ## Why Pro Metal Drummers Choose the Tama Star Classic Maple

The Star Classic Maple is engineered for live performance at the highest level. Key reasons metal drummers trust this kit include:

- **Star-Cast mounting system**: eliminates shell contact, allowing resonance to ring fully even under heavy attack
- **Die-cast hoops**: produce a sharp, focused rimshot that cuts through high-volume band mixes
- **Bearing edge precision**: Tama's machine-cut bearing edges deliver consistent attack across every shell size
- **Shell depth options**: available in standard and deeper configurations, letting drummers dial in more attack or more punch
- **Finish durability**: high-gloss lacquer finishes survive touring without the chipping common in lighter-duty kits

Estimated street price for a standard Star Classic Maple configuration: €4,000–€6,000 (shell pack).

## FAQ

**Q: Which pro metal drummers use the Tama Star Classic Maple?**
A: The most prominent metal drummers playing the Tama Star Classic Maple in MetalForge's database are Lars Ulrich (Metallica) and Jay Weinberg (Slipknot). Lars Ulrich has used the kit for Metallica's live performances since the mid-2010s, while Jay Weinberg used it during his 2014–2023 run with Slipknot.

**Q: Is the Tama Star Classic Maple good for metal drumming?**
A: Yes — it is one of the premier choices for high-intensity metal performance. The Star Classic Maple's die-cast hoops, Star-Cast isolation mounting, and all-maple shells produce the focused attack and controlled sustain that metal drummers need at stage volume. Lars Ulrich, among the most visible thrash metal drummers in the world, has trusted this kit for major world tours.

**Q: How does the Tama Star Classic Maple compare to the Starclassic Maple?**
A: The Star Classic Maple is Tama's premium flagship line, featuring the Star-Cast mounting hardware and hand-selected maple shells with tighter tolerances than the standard Starclassic Maple. The Star Classic line commands a higher price point and is aimed at professional-level touring and recording applications. Both lines use all-maple construction, but the Star Classic delivers more refined hardware, better hardware isolation, and more finish options.
