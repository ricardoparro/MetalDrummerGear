# Tama Star Classic Maple Drummers — Which Metal Drummers Use the Tama Star Classic Maple?

> MetalForge's guide to professional metal drummers who play the Tama Star Classic Maple drum kit.
> Last updated: 2026-06-25

The Tama Star Classic Maple (STAR Classic Maple, or "STAR CL") is Tama's flagship domestic-production maple shell series, introduced in the early 2000s as the successor to the Starclassic Maple line. Constructed from 100% maple shells with Tama's proprietary Star-Cast mounting system, the series is engineered for maximum resonance and minimal shell contact — a design that lets each drum sustain freely and respond to nuanced dynamics at any volume. In metal, the STAR Classic Maple's clear, bright attack and extended sustain make it a premium choice for drummers who demand natural acoustic performance on massive stages.

## About the Tama Star Classic Maple

Tama's STAR Classic Maple sits at the top of the brand's production acoustic range. Key characteristics for metal drummers:

- **Shell construction:** 100% maple, 6-ply — natural resonance, clear attack, musical sustain
- **Star-Cast mounting:** Chrome or black mounting system that suspends the shell at two points, eliminating the resonance-choking contact of traditional lug mounting
- **Hardware:** Tama's Starphonic line in stainless steel or black nickel; precision-machined cast lugs
- **Bearing edges:** 45° with a small round-over — centres the head, maximises head-to-bearing-edge contact for consistent resonance
- **Finish options:** Wide custom palette including solids, lacquers, sparkles, and wraps; endorsed artists often commission unique finishes
- **Price range:** Approximately $3,500–$6,000+ (complete shell kit) depending on configuration and finish

The STAR Classic Maple is a natural stage choice for players who have outgrown mid-tier kits and require both acoustic excellence and road reliability.
