## Gear Timeline

### Kublai Khan TX Founding Era (2009–2016)

- **Drums:** Entry-level maple shell pack, 22" kick, 10"/12" rack toms, 14"/16" floor toms
- **Snare:** Standard steel snare, 14"x5.5"
- **Cymbals:** Budget-tier crash/ride/hi-hat set
- **Pedal:** Single chain-drive pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth American Classic 2B
- **Heads:** Standard coated batters
- **Estimated cost (2009–2016):** ~$1,200
- **Notable:** Co-founded Kublai Khan TX in Sherman, Texas, on entry-level gear typical of the DIY Texas hardcore scene, building the groove-first, power-through-restraint style that would define the band.

### Nomad / Rise Records Era (2017–2019)

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums — American maple shells, 22" kick, 10"/12" rack toms, 14"/16" floor toms
- **Snare:** SJC Custom 14"x6.5" Maple
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Classics Custom Dark Series — 14" Hi-Hats, 18"/20" Crashes, 21" Ride, 18" China
- **Pedal:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth American Classic 2B
- **Heads:** Evans Hydraulic
- **Estimated cost (2017–2019):** ~$4,200
- **Notable:** The Rise Records signing that brought Nomad (2017) came with a gear upgrade to boutique SJC Custom Drums and a complete Meinl Classics Custom Dark cymbal setup.

### Absolute Era (2019–2023)

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums, custom American maple shell pack
- **Snare:** SJC Custom 14"x6.5" Maple
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Classics Custom Dark Series — 14" Hi-Hats, 18"/20" Crashes, 21" Ride, 18" China
- **Pedal:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth American Classic 2B
- **Heads:** Evans Hydraulic
- **Original setup cost (2019):** ~$5,619
- **Inflation-adjusted to 2026:** ~$7,199
- **Notable:** Absolute (2019) is widely cited as Kublai Khan TX's defining statement and Lamb's most refined performance, recorded on the SJC Custom / Meinl Classics Custom Dark / DW 9000 rig that became his signature setup.

### Post-Absolute Touring / Current Setup (2023–present)

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums, mature configuration
- **Snare:** SJC Custom 14"x6.5" Maple, maintained
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Classics Custom Dark Series
- **Pedal:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth American Classic 2B
- **Heads:** Evans Hydraulic
- **Estimated cost (current):** ~$6,300
- **Notable:** Setup value climbs with continued international touring and boutique gear market appreciation as Lamb remains the only drummer in Kublai Khan TX's history.

## FAQ

**Q: How much does Isaac Lamb's drum kit cost?**
A: Isaac Lamb's 2019 Absolute-era SJC Custom setup cost approximately $5,619. Adjusted for 2026 inflation, that's equivalent to roughly $7,199. His current setup is valued at approximately $6,300 at current retail.

**Q: What drums does Isaac Lamb play?**
A: Isaac Lamb plays SJC Custom Drums, hand-built to order in Marlborough, Massachusetts. The American maple shells deliver attack clarity for aggressive metalcore passages and enough low-end body for breakdowns to register as physical events — exactly what beatdown hardcore demands.

**Q: What cymbals does Isaac Lamb use?**
A: Isaac Lamb plays a complete Meinl Classics Custom Dark Series setup — 14" hi-hats, 18"/20" crashes, a 21" ride, and an 18" china. The darker, complex tonal character sits naturally within Kublai Khan TX's dense, downtuned guitar frequencies.

**Q: What band is Isaac Lamb the drummer for?**
A: Isaac Lamb is the founding and only drummer of Kublai Khan TX, a beatdown hardcore/metalcore band from Sherman, Texas formed in 2009. His most acclaimed performance came on Absolute (2019), the band's defining studio statement.
