## Gear Timeline

### The Gray Chapter Era (2014)

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums — 5-piece shell pack, standard finish, 22" bass drum, 10"/12" rack toms, 14"/16" floor toms
- **Snare:** SJC Custom Brass 14"x6.5"
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian A Custom/K mix — 14" A Custom hi-hats, 18" A Custom crashes, 20" K ride
- **Pedal:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vater 5A (stock model)
- **Heads:** Evans G2 Clear (toms/kick), Evans G1 Coated (snare)
- **Original setup cost (2014):** ~$6,542
- **Inflation-adjusted to 2026:** ~$9,054
- **Notable:** Weinberg's debut with Slipknot on ".5: The Gray Chapter," recorded after the death of bassist Paul Gray and after Weinberg replaced Joey Jordison. Years before he had a signature snare or signature stick, his rig was a standard SJC Custom shell pack with stock Vater sticks.

### We Are Not Your Kind Era (2019)

- **Notable:** SJC develops the "Crucible" — a 48-ply brass snare built to Weinberg's spec — cementing his move from stock endorsed gear to a signature product line.

### The End, So Far Era (2022) / Departure (2023)

- **Notable:** Weinberg's final Slipknot studio album. He parted ways with the band in November 2023 and joined Suicidal Tendencies.

### Current Era

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums (OBEY x ST Collaboration Kit)
- **Snare:** SJC Jay Weinberg "The Crucible" 14"x6.5" 48-ply Brass
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian (14" A New Beat hi-hats, 18"/19" A Custom crashes, 21" K Custom ride, 19" K china, 7" FX Break Bell)
- **Hardware:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal, DW 9100 Throne
- **Sticks:** Vater Jay Weinberg 908 Signature
- **Heads:** Evans Black Chrome
- **Notable:** Weinberg's current verified rig centers on a special-edition SJC Custom Drums collaboration kit, his own "Crucible" signature snare, and his own Vater signature stick — a fully signature-branded evolution from the standard shell pack he debuted with in 2014.

## FAQ

**Q: How much does Jay Weinberg's drum kit cost?**  
A: Jay Weinberg's original 2014 SJC Custom/Zildjian setup — the rig behind his Slipknot debut ".5: The Gray Chapter" — cost approximately $6,542. Adjusted for 2026 inflation, that's equivalent to roughly $9,054. His current signature-branded SJC/Vater rig costs meaningfully more, reflecting the premium of a fully custom collaboration kit and signature snare/stick line.

**Q: What kit did Jay Weinberg use in Slipknot?**  
A: Jay Weinberg played an SJC Custom Drums shell pack throughout his nine years with Slipknot (2014–2023), starting with a standard finish and stock Vater sticks in 2014 and evolving into his own SJC "The Crucible" 48-ply brass signature snare and Vater Jay Weinberg 908 Signature stick by the We Are Not Your Kind era.

**Q: Is Jay Weinberg's drum setup affordable?**  
A: Weinberg's 2014 debut-era SJC Custom setup (~$6,542) was a standard boutique shell pack, not a signature-artist rig — comparable to what any working drummer could order from SJC. His current setup, built around his own signature snare, stick, and a special collaboration kit finish, carries a signature-artist premium.

**Q: What drums does Jay Weinberg play now?**  
A: Jay Weinberg's current setup is an SJC Custom Drums OBEY x ST Collaboration Kit with his own SJC "The Crucible" 48-ply brass snare, Zildjian cymbals, a DW 9000 Series double pedal and DW 9100 throne, and his Vater Jay Weinberg 908 Signature sticks — the rig he plays with Suicidal Tendencies.
