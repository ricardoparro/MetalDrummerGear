# Meinl Byzance Series Cymbals

**Category:** cymbals | **Brand:** Meinl

---

## Overview

Meinl Byzance cymbals are hand-hammered in Turkey using traditional B20 bronze alloy. The series offers a wide range of sounds from dark and trashy to bright and cutting. Popular with progressive and extreme metal drummers for their complex overtones and dynamic response.
