# Paiste RUDE Series Cymbals

**Category:** cymbals | **Brand:** Paiste

---

## Overview

Paiste RUDE cymbals are designed specifically for heavy hitting drummers. Made from CuSn8 bronze (2002 Bronze), they deliver cutting, aggressive tones that cut through the loudest stage volumes. A staple in thrash and death metal drumming since the 1980s.
