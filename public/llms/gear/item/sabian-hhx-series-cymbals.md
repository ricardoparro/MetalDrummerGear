# Sabian HHX Series Cymbals

**Category:** cymbals | **Brand:** Sabian

---

## Overview

Sabian HHX cymbals combine hand-hammering with modern manufacturing for a dark, complex sound. The HHX line offers excellent dynamic range and musical warmth, making them popular with progressive and modern metal drummers.
