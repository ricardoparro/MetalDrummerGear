# Sonor SQ2 Heavy Beech Drums

**Category:** drums | **Brand:** Sonor

## Overview

Sonor SQ2 drums with Heavy Beech shells are custom-built in Germany. The dense beech wood delivers powerful projection and sustain, making them ideal for heavy music. The SQ2 system allows complete customization of shell configurations.
