# Tama Iron Cobra 900 Power Glide Double Pedal

**Category:** hardware | **Brand:** Tama

---

## Overview

The Tama Iron Cobra 900 is one of the most popular double bass pedals in metal drumming. Known for its smooth Power Glide cam, quick response, and durability, it has been the pedal of choice for many legendary drummers. The Iron Cobra features a rolling glide design that provides a linear feel throughout the stroke.
