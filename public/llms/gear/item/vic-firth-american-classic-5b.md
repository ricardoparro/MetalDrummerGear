# Vic Firth American Classic 5B Drumsticks

**Category:** sticks | **Brand:** Vic Firth

---

## Overview

The Vic Firth American Classic 5B is one of the most popular drumstick models in the world. Slightly larger than a 5A with a bold sound and full feel, the 5B offers excellent balance and durability. The tear drop tip produces a rich, full tone on cymbals.
