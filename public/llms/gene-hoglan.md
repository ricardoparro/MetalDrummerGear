# Gene Hoglan — Drum Kit, Gear & Technique

Gene Hoglan is the drummer for Death (1993–1995), Testament (2007–present), and Dethklok, widely regarded as one of the most precise and influential drummers in extreme metal history. Nicknamed "The Atomic Clock" for his metronomic accuracy, Hoglan has performed with over 20 bands across four decades.

## Kit Overview

Gene Hoglan plays a Tama Starclassic Walnut/Birch drum kit, the setup behind some of extreme metal's most demanding performances across Death, Testament, Dark Angel, and Dethklok. The Starclassic Walnut/Birch combines warmth and attack — the tonal balance that lets Hoglan navigate the extreme dynamics of death metal and thrash while maintaining the metronomic precision that earned him the nickname "The Atomic Clock."

The cornerstone of the Gene Hoglan drum set is his Tama Gene Hoglan Signature snare drum — an exceptionally deep 14×8" model engineered for thunderous power and cutting attack, defining his blast-beat and groove passages on landmark albums like Death's Symbolic and Testament's Formation of Damnation. Zildjian A Custom Series cymbals complete the drum kit: 15" hi-hats for crisp articulation, 18" and 20" crashes for explosive accents, a 22" ride, and a 20" China. Tama Speed Cobra 910 double bass pedals power the rapid foot passages underpinning his cross-genre technical precision. Vater 5B Wood Tip sticks complete this drum set.
