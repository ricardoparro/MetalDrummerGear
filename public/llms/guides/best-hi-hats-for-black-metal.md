## What Hi-Hats Are Used in Black Metal?

Hi-hats carry an outsized role in black metal — they're the rhythmic engine behind blast beats, tremolo-picked riffs, and the genre's signature wall-of-sound atmosphere. Black metal frequently builds entire passages around a single, relentless hi-hat pattern driving the blast beat underneath.

Hellhammer of Mayhem uses 14" Dark Hi-Hats from the Meinl Byzance Series, giving his foundational black metal recordings a dark, complex wash that suits the genre's icy, lo-fi atmosphere. Frost of Satyricon and 1349 currently relies on Zildjian A Custom & K Series cymbals, balancing cutting articulation with complexity for his hyperspeed blast beats. Inferno of Behemoth uses Paiste RUDE Series 14" Hi-Hats, giving Behemoth's modern black/death hybrid attack explosive projection.

## Dark vs Bright Hi-Hats for Black Metal

**Dark (Meinl Byzance Dark — Hellhammer's choice):**
- Raw, atmospheric, complex overtones
- Suits traditional, lo-fi black metal production
- The historically foundational black metal hi-hat sound

**Bright (Zildjian A Custom & K — Frost's choice; Paiste RUDE — Inferno's choice):**
- More articulate, cutting attack
- Suits modern, technical, and black/death hybrid styles
- The contemporary standard for technical black metal

**Verdict:** Choose dark (Meinl Byzance Dark) for traditional, atmospheric black metal in the Mayhem tradition. Choose bright (Zildjian A Custom & K or Paiste RUDE) for modern, technical, or black/death hybrid styles in the Satyricon or Behemoth tradition.

## FAQ

**What hi-hats are used in black metal?**
Hellhammer of Mayhem uses Meinl Byzance Dark 14" Hi-Hats. Frost of Satyricon and 1349 uses Zildjian A Custom & K Series. Inferno of Behemoth uses Paiste RUDE 14" Hi-Hats. All three are 14" pairs — the genre's standard diameter.

**What hi-hats does Hellhammer use?**
Hellhammer of Mayhem uses 14" Dark Hi-Hats from the Meinl Byzance Series, part of his full Byzance cymbal setup. The dark, complex wash suits the raw, icy atmosphere of Mayhem's foundational recordings.

**Best hi-hats for blast beats?**
Medium-heavy weight hi-hats with consistent bottom-cymbal contact handle sustained blast beat endurance best. Meinl Byzance Dark, Zildjian A Custom & K, and Paiste RUDE all perform well at extreme tempos.

**What size hi-hats for black metal?**
14" is the standard black metal hi-hat size, used by Hellhammer, Frost, and Inferno alike. Some players prefer 13" for marginally faster response at maximum blast beat speeds.

**Dark or bright hi-hats for black metal?**
Both work at the highest level. Dark hi-hats suit traditional, atmospheric black metal. Bright hi-hats suit modern, technical, and black/death hybrid styles. Choose based on subgenre and production style.
