## Ziltoid Goes Home Odd-Meter Polyrhythm

**Song:** Ziltoid Goes Home | **Album:** Z² (2014) | **BPM:** 128 BPM | **Technique:** 7/8 odd meter | **Difficulty:** expert

Hi-hat accents fall on the downbeat of each group, making the meter audible without disrupting flow. The bass drum placement shifts relative to the 7/8 cycle rather than following 4/4 grid logic, and looping the pattern reveals any unevenness that playing linearly hides.
