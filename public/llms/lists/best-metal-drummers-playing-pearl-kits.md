## Overview

Pearl Drums shows up more than almost any other brand name across MetalForge's subgenre lists, from Cannibal Corpse's death metal foundation to Slipknot's nu-metal breakthrough — yet the brand has never had its own ranking. This is Pearl's page: the ten most notable metal drummers built around Pearl Reference, Masters, and Masterworks kits, spanning death metal, technical death metal, progressive metal, and blackened death metal. Where Tama's metal roster leans thrash and groove, Pearl's runs straight through extreme metal's most technical and blast-beat-heavy corners.

The best metal drummers playing Pearl drum kits, ranked. Paul Mazurkiewicz, Joey Jordison, George Kollias, Flo Mounier, Gene Hoglan and more — Pearl's deepest roster in death, extreme, and progressive metal.
