## Overview

The most brutal and technically ferocious percussionists in heavy music. Extreme metal drummers operate at the absolute limit of human capability — blast beats at 280+ BPM, inhuman double bass endurance, and rhythmic complexity that defies physical logic. This list spans death, black, brutal death, technical death, and grindcore to rank the most extreme drummers across all subgenres.

The greatest extreme metal drummers of all time. George Kollias, Flo Mounier, Pete Sandoval, Hellhammer and more — the definitive ranking of death metal, black metal, and brutal metal percussion.
