## Overview

The award-winners, Grammy holders, and critically acclaimed percussionists of heavy metal. This list measures not just talent but recognition — Grammy wins and nominations, Modern Drummer awards, certified platinum records, Rock Hall and Metal Hall of Fame honors, and the international critical consensus that makes careers permanent. From Lars Ulrich's record nine Grammy wins to Danny Carey's acclaimed "Fear Inoculum" performance, these drummers have the trophies to match the talent.

The most decorated metal drummers by Grammy wins, awards, and industry recognition. Lars Ulrich, Danny Carey, Dave Lombardo, Mike Portnoy, Nicko McBrain and more — metal drumming's most awarded and critically acclaimed performers.

## Rankings

Ranked by documented performance records, genre-defining influence, and technical contribution. Top entries: Lars Ulrich, Danny Carey, Dave Lombardo, Mike Portnoy, Nicko McBrain, and more.

### 1. Lars Ulrich

**Band:** Metallica
**Highlight:** 9 Grammy wins — the most decorated drummer in metal history
**Why ranked here:** Lars Ulrich has accumulated more Grammy wins than any other metal drummer. Metallica's haul includes Best Metal Performance wins across multiple decades, a Rock and Roll Hall of Fame induction in 2009, and 125+ million certified albums worldwide — a trophy room no metal drummer comes close to matching

### 2. Danny Carey

**Band:** Tool
**Highlight:** Grammy winner for Best Metal Performance — Tool's "Fear Inoculum"
**Why ranked here:** Danny Carey and Tool won Best Metal Performance at the 63rd Grammy Awards (2021) for "Fear Inoculum," adding institutional recognition to a career of mathematical drumming long acclaimed by musicians — the most prestigious award in metal going to progressive metal's greatest living drummer
