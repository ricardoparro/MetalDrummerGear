## Overview

The percussionists behind metal's epic, Norse-themed fusion of black metal ferocity and folk-metal melody. Viking metal grew directly out of Scandinavian black metal and melodic death metal in the late 1980s and 1990s, trading pure aggression for anthemic hooks, mid-tempo grooves built for mass singalongs, and lyrical themes drawn from Norse mythology and seafaring conquest. Amon Amarth built the genre's modern commercial template, while the Norwegian black metal and Swedish/Finnish melodic death metal scenes it grew from supplied the drumming vocabulary — blast beats deployed for scale and atmosphere rather than pure speed. These ten drummers, drawn from Viking metal itself and the closely related Scandinavian black and melodic death metal lineage it emerged from, define the genre's epic rhythmic foundation.

The greatest Viking metal drummers and their closely related Scandinavian black and melodic death metal lineage. Jocke Wallgren, Hellhammer, Daniel Erlandsson, Daray and more — the definitive ranking of Viking metal's most epic percussionists.
