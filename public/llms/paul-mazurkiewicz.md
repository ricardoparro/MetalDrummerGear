# Paul Mazurkiewicz — Drum Kit, Gear & Technique

Paul Mazurkiewicz (born September 8, 1970, Buffalo, New York) is the co-founding drummer of Cannibal Corpse, the best-selling death metal band of all time (2M+ albums sold), and has recorded every one of the band's studio albums from Eaten Back to Life (1990) through Chaos Horrific (2023) — 35+ years and sixteen studio albums without a single lineup change on drums.

## Kit Overview

Paul Mazurkiewicz plays a Pearl Reference drum kit — dual 22" x 18" kick drums, two rack toms, and two floor toms in maple/African mahogany hybrid shells — paired with a full Meinl Byzance cymbal setup and a Pearl Eliminator double bass pedal. The configuration has been essentially unchanged since Kill (2006), carrying through Evisceration Plague (2009), Torture (2012), A Skeletal Domain (2014), Red Before Black (2017), Violence Unimagined (2021), and Chaos Horrific (2023). His 14"x6.5" Pearl Free-Floating steel snare gives his backbeat the bright, cutting presence needed to stay legible against Cannibal Corpse's down-tuned guitars, and the Eliminator's interchangeable cam system powers his locomotive double-bass technique at the 180–250 BPM range Cannibal Corpse's material demands.

## Studio Albums — Closing the Arc to the Present

- **[Red Before Black (2017)](https://metalforge.io/articles/red-before-black-drum-setup)** — Cannibal Corpse's fourteenth studio album, produced by Erik Rutan at his own Mana Recording Studios in St. Petersburg, Florida. It was the last Cannibal Corpse album Rutan produced purely as an outside collaborator before joining the band on guitar in 2020.
- **[Violence Unimagined (2021)](https://metalforge.io/articles/violence-unimagined-drum-setup)** — the band's fifteenth studio album and the first to feature Erik Rutan as an official band member. It debuted at #6 on the Billboard Top Album Sales chart, Cannibal Corpse's first-ever Top 10 there and best-ever first-week sales (~14,000 copies).
- **[Chaos Horrific (2023)](https://metalforge.io/articles/chaos-horrific-drum-setup)** — Cannibal Corpse's sixteenth and, as of this writing, most recent studio album, produced by Rutan at Mana Recording Studios and released September 22, 2023. It debuted at #1 on the UK Rock & Metal Albums chart.

## Technique Highlights

- **Push-pull blast beat:** Uses gravity and stick rebound rather than pure power striking, sustaining extreme tempos across 90-minute sets
- **Locomotive double-kick:** Kick drums function as an equal rhythmic partner to the snare, not a supporting layer
- **35+ years of consistency:** Same core Pearl Reference/Meinl/Pearl Eliminator rig across Kill (2006) through Chaos Horrific (2023)
- **Co-founding member:** Formed Cannibal Corpse in Buffalo, New York in 1988 and has drummed on every studio album since
