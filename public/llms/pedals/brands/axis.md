# Axis Bass Drum Pedals — Metal Drummer Endorsers

> Axis Percussion was founded in 1990 by drummer and machinist Darrell Johnston specifically to eliminate the flex and lag inherent in chain-drive pedals. Every Axis model is direct drive — no chain or belt at all — which has made the brand an extreme-metal icon among drummers chasing maximum, zero-lag blast-beat speed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What drive type does Axis use?**
A: All Axis pedals are direct drive — a rigid rod connecting footboard to beater with no chain or belt, for the fastest, most consistent response of any drive type.

**Q: Why do extreme metal drummers use Axis pedals?**
A: The direct-drive mechanism removes all mechanical slack between footboard and beater, giving instant, zero-lag response — valuable for sustained blast-beat and extreme-speed patterns. Mayhem's Jan Axel "Hellhammer" Blomberg plays an Axis double pedal.
