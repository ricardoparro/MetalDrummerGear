# Czarcie Kopyto Bass Drum Pedals for Metal

> Czarcie Kopyto ("Devil's Hoof") is a Polish boutique pedal maker whose CNC-machined, hand-assembled pedals — nicknamed the "Polish Tank" for their durability — are verified on the roster through Inferno (Behemoth) and, under the Monolit model name, Adrian Erlandsson (At the Gates, The Haunted).

---

## Company Background

- Independent; hand-built in Poland

## Metal-Relevant Models

- **Monolit:** A hand-built, CNC-machined double pedal with an independently sprung slave pedal — the model verified on the roster through Adrian Erlandsson (At the Gates, The Haunted).
