## Notable Pearl Pedal Lines

- **Demon Drive** — Direct-drive double pedal, a rigid rod with no chain or belt, giving the fastest, most consistent response of any drive type; the most common Pearl pedal on the verified roster.
- **Demon XR** — A co-designed evolution of the Demon Drive, direct drive, used by technical-death-metal specialists chasing sustained blast-beat speed.
- **Eliminator** — Chain-drive double pedal, Pearl's more traditional-feel option alongside its direct-drive Demon line.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the Pearl Demon Drive chain or direct drive?**
A: Direct drive — a rigid rod connecting footboard to beater with no chain or belt, for the fastest, most consistent response of any drive type.

**Q: What pedal do most Pearl-endorsed metal drummers use?**
A: The Demon Drive is the most common, played by 9 of the 14 verified Pearl pedal users on MetalForge, including Joey Jordison, Gene Hoglan, and Mike Mangini.

**Q: What's the difference between Pearl Demon Drive and Demon XR?**
A: The Demon XR is a co-designed evolution of the direct-drive Demon Drive mechanism — George Kollias (Nile) co-designed the XR variant.
