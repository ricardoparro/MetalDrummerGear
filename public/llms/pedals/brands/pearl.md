# Pearl Bass Drum Pedals for Metal

> Pearl's pedal lineup splits into two verified-roster standards: the chain-drive Eliminator, and the direct-drive Demon Drive (and its Demon XR sibling) built specifically for speed and durability under heavy touring use. Demon Drive in particular is the extreme-metal and technical-metal default on this roster, played by drummers from Gene Hoglan and Joey Jordison to Mike Mangini.

---

## Company Background

- **Founded:** 1946, Tokyo, Japan
- Independent; one of the largest drum manufacturers in the world

## Metal-Relevant Models

- **Demon Drive:** Direct drive, engineered for maximum speed — the most common single pedal model on the verified extreme/technical metal roster, from Gene Hoglan to Mike Mangini.
- **Demon XR:** A direct-drive evolution of the Demon Drive, co-designed with roster drummer George Kollias.
- **Eliminator:** Chain drive — a more traditional-feel double pedal used by drummers including Paul Bostaph and Jaska Raatikainen.
