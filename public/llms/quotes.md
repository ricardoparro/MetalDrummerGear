## Lars Ulrich (Metallica)

> "I'm not the best drummer in the world, but I'm the best drummer for Metallica." — Lars Ulrich (Metallica)
> *Modern Drummer Magazine, 2008 · Topic: philosophy*

> "The only way to do great work is to love what you do. I've been fortunate enough to do that for over 40 years." — Lars Ulrich (Metallica)
> *Drumeo Interview, 2020 · Topic: career*

> "Music is the most powerful form of communication in the world. It brings people together from all walks of life." — Lars Ulrich (Metallica)
> *Rolling Stone, 2016 · Topic: philosophy*
