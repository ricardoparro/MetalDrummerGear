# What Snare Does Chris Adler Use? Mapex Chris Adler Signature Walnut/Maple

**Band:** Lamb of God | **Brand:** Mapex | **Model:** Chris Adler Signature Walnut/Maple

---

## Direct Answer

Chris Adler plays the Mapex Chris Adler Signature Walnut/Maple — a signature snare built for them, sized 14x5.5" with a Maple/Walnut shell.

This is a namesake signature model — Mapex built the Chris Adler Signature Walnut/Maple specifically for Chris Adler of Lamb of God, rather than Chris Adler simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell. That distinction is why this snare gets its own page: it's the drummer's own spec, not a generic stock configuration another drummer could also be playing.
