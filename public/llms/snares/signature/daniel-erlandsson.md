# What Snare Does Daniel Erlandsson Use? Pearl Daniel Erlandsson Signature

**Band:** Arch Enemy | **Brand:** Pearl | **Model:** Daniel Erlandsson Signature

## Direct Answer

Daniel Erlandsson plays the Pearl Daniel Erlandsson Signature — a signature snare built for them, sized 14x5.5".

This is a namesake signature model — Pearl built the Daniel Erlandsson Signature specifically for Daniel Erlandsson of Arch Enemy, rather than Daniel Erlandsson simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell. That distinction is why this snare gets its own page: it's the drummer's own spec, not a generic stock configuration another drummer could also be playing.
