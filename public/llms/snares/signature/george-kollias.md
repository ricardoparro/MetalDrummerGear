# What Snare Does George Kollias Use? Pearl George Kollias Signature

**Band:** Nile | **Brand:** Pearl | **Model:** George Kollias Signature

---

## Direct Answer

George Kollias plays the Pearl George Kollias Signature — a signature snare built for them, sized 14x6.5".

This is a namesake signature model — Pearl built the George Kollias Signature specifically for George Kollias of Nile, rather than George Kollias simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell. That distinction is why this snare gets its own page: it's the drummer's own spec, not a generic stock configuration another drummer could also be playing.
