# What Snare Does Joey Jordison Use? Pearl Joey Jordison Signature

**Band:** Slipknot | **Brand:** Pearl | **Model:** Joey Jordison Signature

---

## Direct Answer

Joey Jordison plays the Pearl Joey Jordison Signature — a signature snare built for them, sized 13x6.5".

This is a namesake signature model — Pearl built the Joey Jordison Signature specifically for Joey Jordison of Slipknot, rather than Joey Jordison simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell. That distinction is why this snare gets its own page: it's the drummer's own spec, not a generic stock configuration another drummer could also be playing.
