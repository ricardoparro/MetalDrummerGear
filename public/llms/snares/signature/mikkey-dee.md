# What Snare Does Mikkey Dee Use? Yamaha Mikkey Dee Signature

**Band:** Scorpions / Motörhead | **Brand:** Yamaha | **Model:** Mikkey Dee Signature

---

## Direct Answer

Mikkey Dee plays the Yamaha Mikkey Dee Signature — a signature snare built for them, sized 14x8".

This is a namesake signature model — Yamaha built the Mikkey Dee Signature specifically for Mikkey Dee of Scorpions / Motörhead, rather than Mikkey Dee simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell. That distinction is why this snare gets its own page: it's the drummer's own spec, not a generic stock configuration another drummer could also be playing.
