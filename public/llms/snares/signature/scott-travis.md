## Direct Answer

Scott Travis plays the ddrum Scott Travis Signature — a signature snare built for them, sized 14x6.5".

This is a namesake signature model — ddrum built the Scott Travis Signature specifically for Scott Travis of Judas Priest, rather than Scott Travis simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell.
