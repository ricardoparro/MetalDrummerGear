# What Snare Does Tomas Haake Use? Sonor Tomas Haake Signature & Artist Series Bronze

## Direct Answer

Tomas Haake plays the Sonor Tomas Haake Signature & Artist Series Bronze — a signature snare built for them, sized 14x6.5" with a Bronze shell.

This is a namesake signature model — Sonor built the Tomas Haake Signature & Artist Series Bronze specifically for Tomas Haake of Meshuggah, rather than Tomas Haake simply endorsing an off-the-shelf shell. That distinction is why this snare gets its own page: it's the drummer's own spec, not a generic stock configuration another drummer could also be playing.
