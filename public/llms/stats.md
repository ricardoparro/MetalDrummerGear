## Gear Preferences by Genre

Observed patterns from MetalForge's genre-tagged roster:

- **Death Metal / Technical Death Metal:** Pearl drums and Sabian cymbals are dominant. Double bass pedals: near-universal (100% in the database subset).
- **Thrash Metal:** Tama drums, Zildjian or Paiste cymbals. DW pedals widely used.
- **Black Metal:** Mix of Tama, Sonor, and Pearl. Paiste cymbals popular for the raw, cutting tone needed in black metal production.
- **Progressive / Djent:** Sonor SQ2 and Pearl Reference kits appear frequently. Meinl Byzance cymbals are the genre standout (10 of 10 Meinl users lean prog/djent/death).
- **Nu Metal / Groove Metal:** Pearl and Tama split evenly. DW hardware most common.
