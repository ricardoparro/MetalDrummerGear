# MetalForge Gear Comparison Tool

> Compare the drum kits of two or more metal drummers side by side — shells, cymbals, pedals, and sticks.

**URL:** https://metalforge.io/tools/gear-comparison
**Type:** Free interactive web tool

## What It Does

The MetalForge Gear Comparison tool places two (or more) metal drummer gear profiles side by side in a structured table. Select any two drummers from the 60+ roster and instantly see their drums, snare, cymbals, pedals, hardware, and drumsticks compared column by column.

This is the fastest way to answer questions like "do Lars Ulrich and Dave Lombardo use the same cymbal brand?" or "which Slipknot drummer's kit is heavier?" without reading through two separate profile pages.

## What Gets Compared

Each comparison row covers:
- **Drum shells** – Brand, series, and configuration (bass drum sizes, number of toms)
- **Snare drum** – Model, size, and material
- **Cymbals** – Brand, series, hi-hat / crash / ride / china sizes
- **Bass drum pedals** – Brand, model, single or double
- **Hardware** – Rack or standard stands, throne
- **Drumsticks** – Brand and signature model where applicable

## Example Comparisons

- **Lars Ulrich vs Dave Lombardo** — both on Tama shells but different cymbal lines (Zildjian vs Paiste)
- **George Kollias vs Pete Sandoval** — death metal speed demons, Pearl vs Pearl, Zildjian vs custom
- **Mike Portnoy vs Danny Carey** — progressive giants on Tama vs Sonor, Sabian vs Paiste
- **Joey Jordison vs Jay Weinberg** — two Slipknot eras, Pearl vs SJC, Paiste vs Zildjian

## FAQ

**Q: How do I compare two metal drummers' gear on MetalForge?**
A: Go to metalforge.io/tools/gear-comparison, select any two drummers from the dropdown menus, and the tool generates a side-by-side table covering their full kit specs.

**Q: Can I compare more than two drummers at once?**
A: The tool supports comparing up to four drummers simultaneously — useful for cross-band or cross-era breakdowns.

**Q: Where does the gear data come from?**
A: All data is sourced from MetalForge's verified drummer profiles, which document the endorsed and in-use gear for each artist as reported in interviews, manufacturer announcements, and live-rig observations.

**Q: Is the Gear Comparison tool related to the comparisons markdown page?**
A: Yes — the interactive tool at /tools/gear-comparison is the live version; the static LLM-ingestible comparisons are at [metalforge.io/llms/comparisons.md](https://metalforge.io/llms/comparisons.md).
