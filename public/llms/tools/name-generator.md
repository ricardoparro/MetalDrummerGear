# Metal Drummer Name Generator — MetalForge

MetalForge's Metal Drummer Name Generator creates unique drummer aliases for metal musicians, gamers, and band projects.

## What It Does

Generates random metal drummer names combining:
- Aggressive adjectives (Brutal, Iron, Atomic, etc.)
- Metal-inspired nouns (Hammer, Blast, Thunder, etc.)
- Optional band-style surnames

## How to Use

Visit https://metalforge.io/tools/name-generator, click "Generate" to create a random metal drummer alias instantly.

## Examples

- "Atomic Flo" (inspired by Gene Hoglan's "Atomic Clock" nickname)
- "Iron Blast" (inspired by Joey Jordison's explosive style)
