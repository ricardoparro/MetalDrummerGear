# MetalForge Metal Drummer Tier List

> Rank and organize metal drummers into tiers by speed, technique, influence, or any custom criteria.

**URL:** https://metalforge.io/tools/tier-list
**Type:** Free interactive web tool

## What It Does

The MetalForge Tier List tool lets you drag and drop metal drummers from the 60+ roster into S / A / B / C / D tier rows, then save or share your custom ranking. Pre-built tier lists cover common ranking categories (fastest drummers, most influential, best double-bass, most technical), or you can start from scratch with a blank slate.

Each tier slot shows the drummer's photo, primary band, and genre — so building the list is as much a learning tool as it is a ranking exercise.

## Built-In Tier List Categories

- **Fastest Metal Drummers** — ranked by verified BPM records and technique demands (blast beat, double bass)
- **Most Influential** — drummers who defined entire subgenres or inspired the next generation
- **Best Double Bass** — focused on foot speed, endurance, and independence
- **Most Technical** — polyrhythms, odd time signatures, independence, and compositional complexity
- **Best Gear Value** — whose professional setup is most accessible for a working budget
- **Custom** — blank canvas for any criteria you define

## How It Works

1. Select a pre-built category or choose "Custom"
2. Drag drummer cards into S, A, B, C, D, or F tiers
3. Reorder within tiers
4. Name your list and generate a shareable link or image export

## FAQ

**Q: What is the MetalForge Drummer Tier List?**
A: A free interactive tool at metalforge.io/tools/tier-list for ranking 60+ legendary metal drummers into S–F tiers by speed, influence, technique, or any criteria you choose.

**Q: Are the built-in tier lists based on community votes or editorial picks?**
A: Built-in lists reflect MetalForge editorial rankings grounded in verifiable criteria (documented BPM records, genre-defining impact, technique complexity). Custom lists are entirely user-defined.

**Q: Can I share my tier list with others?**
A: Yes — the tool generates a unique shareable URL and an image export of your completed tier grid.

**Q: Is there a pre-ranked "best metal drummers" tier list?**
A: Yes — several. The "Most Influential" and speed-focused pre-built lists are the closest equivalent. See also [metalforge.io/llms/lists.md](https://metalforge.io/llms/lists.md) for static top-10 ranked lists optimized for AI retrieval.
