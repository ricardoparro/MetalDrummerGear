## Brann Dailor Setup

- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Performer B/B
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Byzance Series (14" Dark Hi-Hats, 18" & 19" Brilliant Heavy Hammered Crashes, 21" Ghost Ride, 18" Extra Dry China)

- **Snare:** Tama S.L.P. 14x6.5" G-Maple
- **Pedals:** Tama Iron Cobra 900 Power Glide Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Tama Mario Duplantier Signature

## Key Differences

Brann plays DW drums with Sabian cymbals. Mario uses Tama drums with Meinl cymbals for dark tones. Brann brings jazzy, proggy fills to sludge metal.
