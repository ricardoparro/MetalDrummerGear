## Playing Style

Brann Dailor uses drumming as a melodic texture, weaving flowing fills and jazz-influenced patterns into Mastodon's sludge metal. Mike Portnoy delivers technique-as-spectacle progressive metal with theatrical fills and dramatic arrangement complexity.
