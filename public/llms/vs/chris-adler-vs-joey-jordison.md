# Chris Adler vs Joey Jordison — Drum Kit Comparison

> Side-by-side gear comparison between Chris Adler (Lamb of God) and Joey Jordison (Slipknot).

Lamb of God's Chris Adler vs Slipknot's Joey Jordison. Groove metal vs nu-metal intensity compared. Two defining drummers of 2000s metal.

## Chris Adler Setup

- **Drums:** Mapex Black Panther Design Lab
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Byzance Series (14" Dark Hi-Hats, 18" & 19" Dark Crashes, 21" Transition Ride, 18" Extra Dry China)
- **Snare:** Mapex Chris Adler Signature 14x5.5" Walnut/Maple
- **Pedals:** Mapex Falcon Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark TX5AXW Chris Adler Signature

## Joey Jordison Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series
- **Cymbals:** Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series (14" Wild Hi-Hats, 16", 17", 18", 19" Power Crashes, 20" & 22" Wild Chinas, 22" Power Ride)
- **Snare:** Pearl Joey Jordison Signature 13x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark Joey Jordison Signature TX515W

## Key Differences

Chris played DW drums with Meinl cymbals for dark attack. Joey endorsed Pearl drums with Paiste cymbals for aggressive tone. Chris defined modern groove metal with Lamb of God's aggressive yet technical approach. Joey brought extreme metal intensity to mainstream nu-metal with Slipknot. Chris mastered precision groove drumming with signature triplet patterns. Joey combined death metal speed with theatrical showmanship.
