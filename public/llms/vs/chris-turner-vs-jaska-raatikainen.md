## Playing Style

Chris Turner drives Oceans Ate Alaska's progressive metalcore with polyrhythmic, fusion-influenced patterns and Japanese-inspired arrangements. Jaska Raatikainen anchored Children of Bodom's melodic death metal with driving double bass and neoclassical-tinged aggression across 26 years.

Turner incorporates complex time signatures and intricate blast beats influenced by Matt Halpern and Matt Garstka, pushing modern metalcore's technical ceiling. Raatikainen built his reputation on rapid, fluid double bass volleys and precise blast beats that anchored Alexi Laiho's dense guitar arrangements.
