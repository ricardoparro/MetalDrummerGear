## Gene Hoglan Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Pure
- **Cymbals:** Sabian AAX Series (15" Hi-Hats, 18" & 20" Crashes, 22" Ride, 20" China)
- **Snare:** Pearl Reference 14x6.5" Brass
- **Pedals:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark 5B

## George Kollias Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Masterworks Stadium Exotic (Piano Black with Gold Hardware)
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian (14" K Mastersound Hi-Hats, 17" & 18" K Custom Dark Crashes, 21" A Custom Mega Bell Ride, 18" China)
- **Snare:** Pearl George Kollias Signature 14x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Pearl Demon XR Double Pedal (Co-designed)
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth George Kollias Signature SGK

## Key Differences

Gene endorses Pearl drums for power and projection with signature sticks. George plays Pearl drums with Pearl Demon Drive pedals for ultimate speed. Gene brings scientific precision to progressive death and thrash metal with inhuman timing. George delivers Egyptian-themed technical brutality with sustained extreme tempos. Gene pioneered the gravity blast and is known for impossibly tight double bass and timing. George uses heel-toe technique for sustained blast beats at 280+ BPM.
