# Joey Jordison vs Dave Lombardo — Drum Kit Comparison

> Side-by-side gear comparison between Joey Jordison (Slipknot) and Dave Lombardo (Slayer).

Slipknot's Joey Jordison vs Slayer's Dave Lombardo. Nu-metal intensity vs thrash metal pioneering compared. Two extreme drumming icons.

## Joey Jordison Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series
- **Cymbals:** Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series (14" Wild Hi-Hats, 16", 17", 18", 19" Power Crashes, 20" & 22" Wild Chinas, 22" Power Ride)
- **Snare:** Pearl Joey Jordison Signature 13x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark Joey Jordison Signature TX515W

## Dave Lombardo Setup

- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Maple
- **Cymbals:** Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series (15" Sound Edge Hi-Hats, 18" & 19" Crashes, 22" Reign Power Ride, 18" China)
- **Snare:** Tama S.L.P. 14x6.5" G-Maple
- **Pedals:** Tama Iron Cobra 900 Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark Dave Lombardo Signature 2Bx

## Key Differences

Joey played Pearl drums with Paiste cymbals. Dave uses Pearl drums with Paiste RUDE cymbals for aggressive cut. Joey brought extreme metal intensity to mainstream nu-metal with blast beats and speed. Dave pioneered thrash metal drumming with relentless aggression. Joey combined death metal speed with theatrical showmanship on a rotating kit. Dave revolutionized double bass and blast beats in thrash.
