## Joey Jordison Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series
- **Cymbals:** Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series (14" Wild Hi-Hats, 16", 17", 18", 19" Power Crashes, 20" & 22" Wild Chinas, 22" Power Ride)
- **Snare:** Pearl Joey Jordison Signature 13x6.5"

- **Sticks:** Promark Joey Jordison Signature TX515W

## Jay Weinberg Setup

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums (OBEY x ST Collaboration Kit)
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian (14" A New Beat Hi-Hats, 18" & 19" A Custom Crashes, 21" K Custom Ride, 19" K China, 7" FX Break Bell)
- **Snare:** SJC Jay Weinberg "The Crucible" 14x6.5" 48-ply Brass
- **Pedals:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vater Jay Weinberg 908 Signature

## Key Differences

Joey played Pearl drums with Paiste cymbals. Jay uses Pearl Masters with Zildjian A Custom cymbals for aggressive attack. Joey defined Slipknot's chaotic, extreme drumming style with blast beats and theatrical intensity. Jay brought a more groove-oriented approach while honoring Joey's legacy. Joey pioneered the rotating drum platform and extreme speed in nu-metal. Jay delivers powerful, precise playing with massive groove and dynamic control.
