## Lars Ulrich Setup

- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Maple
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian A Custom Series (14" Dyno Beat Hi-Hats, 16", 17" & 18" Rock Crashes, 20" Z Custom China, 22" Ride)
- **Snare:** Tama LU1465 Lars Ulrich Signature 14x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Tama Iron Cobra 900 Power Glide Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Ahead Lars Ulrich Signature Drumsticks

## Dave Lombardo Setup

- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Maple
- **Cymbals:** Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series (15" Sound Edge Hi-Hats, 18" & 19" Crashes, 22" Reign Power Ride, 18" China)
- **Snare:** Tama S.L.P. 14x6.5" G-Maple
- **Pedals:** Tama Iron Cobra 900 Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark Dave Lombardo Signature 2Bx

## Key Differences

Both endorse Tama drums. Lars uses Zildjian cymbals for bright attack; Dave prefers Paiste RUDE for aggressive cut. Lars brings groove-oriented thrash with iconic mid-tempo stomps. Dave delivers relentless speed and technical precision with blast beat pioneering. Lars favors powerful, punchy patterns with signature fills. Dave revolutionized double bass in thrash with surgical timing and speed.
