## Lars Ulrich Setup

- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Maple
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian A Custom Series (14" Dyno Beat Hi-Hats, 16", 17" & 18" Rock Crashes, 20" Z Custom China, 22" Ride)
- **Snare:** Tama LU1465 Lars Ulrich Signature 14x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Tama Iron Cobra 900 Power Glide Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Ahead Lars Ulrich Signature Drumsticks

## Joey Jordison Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series
- **Cymbals:** Paiste RUDE & 2002 Series (14" Wild Hi-Hats, 16", 17", 18", 19" Power Crashes, 20" & 22" Wild Chinas, 22" Power Ride)
- **Snare:** Pearl Joey Jordison Signature 13x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark Joey Jordison Signature TX515W

## Key Differences

Lars plays Tama Starclassic with Zildjian cymbals and Ahead sticks. Joey endorsed Pearl drums with Paiste cymbals for aggressive attack. Lars defined accessible thrash with groove-oriented power. Joey brought extreme metal intensity to mainstream nu-metal with blast beats and speed. Lars favors mid-tempo stomps with powerful fills and iconic ride patterns. Joey combined death metal speed with theatrical showmanship on a rotating drum kit.
