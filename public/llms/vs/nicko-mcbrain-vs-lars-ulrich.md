## Nicko McBrain Setup

- **Drums:** Sonor SQ2 Series
- **Cymbals:** Paiste 2002 & Signature Series (14" Sound Edge Hi-Hats, 16" & 18" Power Crashes, 22" Power Ride, 20" China)
- **Snare:** Sonor Nicko McBrain Signature 14x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Single Bass Drum Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth Nicko McBrain Signature

## Lars Ulrich Setup

- **Drums:** Tama Starclassic Maple
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian A Custom Series (14" Dyno Beat Hi-Hats, 16", 17" & 18" Rock Crashes, 20" Z Custom China, 22" Ride)
- **Snare:** Tama LU1465 Lars Ulrich Signature 14x6.5"
- **Pedals:** Tama Iron Cobra 900 Power Glide Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Ahead Lars Ulrich Signature Drumsticks

## Key Differences

Nicko plays Sonor drums with Paiste cymbals for bright, cutting tone. Lars uses Tama Starclassic with Zildjian cymbals. Nicko brings galloping rhythms and technical precision to Iron Maiden's progressive metal. Lars defined accessible thrash with groove-oriented power. Nicko is known for complex patterns, signature gallops, and single-bass mastery. Lars favors powerful, punchy patterns with iconic ride patterns.
