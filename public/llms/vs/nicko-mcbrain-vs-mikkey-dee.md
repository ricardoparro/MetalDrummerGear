## Playing Style

Nicko delivers galloping rhythms and complex patterns that underpin Iron Maiden's progressive metal epics. Mikkey brings explosive raw power and relentless drive to Motörhead's high-speed rock and roll.

## Technique

Nicko is a single-bass master known for intricate galloping patterns and dynamic control across long songs. Mikkey employs double bass for thunderous speed, hard-hitting snare, and a no-frills aggressive attack.
