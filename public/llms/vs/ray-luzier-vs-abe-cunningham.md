## Ray Luzier Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series
- **Cymbals:** Sabian AAX Series (14" Stage Hi-Hats, 18" & 19" X-Plosion Crashes, 21" Stage Ride, 18" AAXtreme China)
- **Snare:** Pearl Reference 14x6.5" Brass
- **Pedals:** DW 9002 Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth Ray Luzier Signature

## Abe Cunningham Setup

- **Drums:** SJC Custom Drums
- **Cymbals:** Zildjian K Custom & A Custom Series (14" K Custom Hi-Hats, 18" & 20" K Custom Crashes, 22" K Custom Ride, 19" A Custom China)
- **Snare:** SJC Custom 14x6.5" Brass
- **Pedals:** DW 9000 Series Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Vic Firth American Classic 2B

## Key Differences

Ray endorses Pearl drums with Zildjian cymbals. Abe plays DW drums with Zildjian for warm tones. Ray brings session musician precision to Korn's heavy grooves. Abe delivers atmospheric, dynamic playing with emotional depth. Ray excels at tight, syncopated patterns with David Silveria's legacy. Abe masters dynamics and textural playing.
