## Tomas Haake Setup

- **Drums:** Sonor SQ2 Heavy Beech (24"x18" Bass, 10"x8", 12"x9", 13"x10", 16"x14", 18"x16" Toms)
- **Cymbals:** Sabian HHX & AAX Series (14" HHX Compression Hi-Hats, 15" Artisan Hi-Hats, 19" & 20" & 21" HHX Stage Crashes, 22" Legacy Ride, 19" AAXtreme China)
- **Snare:** Sonor Tomas Haake Signature 14x6.5" & Artist Series Bronze
- **Pedals:** Tama Speed Cobra Single Pedals (x2)
- **Sticks:** Wincent Tomas Haake Signature

## Matt Halpern Setup

- **Drums:** Pearl Reference Series
- **Cymbals:** Meinl Byzance Series (15" Dark Hi-Hats, 18" & 20" Extra Dry Medium Crashes, 22" Transition Ride, 18" Extra Dry China)
- **Snare:** Pearl Reference 14x6.5" Brass
- **Pedals:** Pearl Demon Drive Double Pedal
- **Sticks:** Promark Matt Halpern Signature

## Key Differences

Tomas plays Sonor drums with Sabian cymbals for focused attack. Matt uses Pearl drums with Meinl cymbals for dark, complex tones. Tomas invented the djent drumming template with Meshuggah's polyrhythmic complexity. Matt refined and evolved the style for modern progressive metal. Tomas employs machine-like precision with robotic feel and complex time signatures. Matt combines groove-focused polyrhythms with exceptional ghost notes.
