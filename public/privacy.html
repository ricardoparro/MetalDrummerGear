Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

MetalForge (“we”, “us”) operates metalforge.io, a website about metal drummers and their gear. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Usage & device data (analytics). When you visit, we collect standard, non-identifying analytics — pages viewed, approximate region, device/browser type, and referral source — to understand and improve the site.
  • Email address (only if you subscribe). If you submit your email to our updates form, we store it to send you occasional updates and notify the site owner of the signup. You can ask us to remove it at any time.
  • We do not ask for or store payment information, and we do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising:

  • Google Analytics — measures site traffic and usage. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — serves ads. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Advertising & your choices

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info/choices (or youronlinechoices.eu in the EU).
  • Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings.

Affiliate links

Some links to gear retailers are affiliate links. If you buy through them we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This does not influence which gear we feature.

Third-party content

Pages may embed third-party content such as YouTube videos (loaded via youtube-nocookie.com where possible) and images. Those providers may set their own cookies, governed by their privacy policies.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., the EU/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Children

MetalForge is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version.

Contact

Questions or data requests: privacy@metalforge.io.
